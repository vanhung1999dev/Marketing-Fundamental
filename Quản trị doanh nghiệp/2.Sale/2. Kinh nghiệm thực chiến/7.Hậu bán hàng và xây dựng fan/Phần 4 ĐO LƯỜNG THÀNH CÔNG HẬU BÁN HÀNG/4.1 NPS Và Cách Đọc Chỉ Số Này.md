### NPS Và Cách Đọc Chỉ Số Này Đúng Cách

NPS — Net Promoter Score — là metric đơn giản nhất và được dùng nhiều nhất để đo customer loyalty. Câu hỏi: "Trên thang 0-10, anh/chị có khả năng giới thiệu [company] cho bạn bè hoặc đồng nghiệp không?" Score 9-10 = Promoters; 7-8 = Passives; 0-6 = Detractors. NPS = % Promoters - % Detractors.

**Đọc NPS đúng cách:**

*Benchmark:* NPS dưới 0 = poor; 0-30 = average; 30-70 = good; 70+ = excellent. Industry benchmarks vary significantly — 30 may be excellent in telecom, mediocre in SaaS.

*Trend matters more than absolute:* NPS of 40 trending up is better than NPS of 50 trending down. Monitor trend over time, không chỉ snapshot.

*Segment analysis:* Aggregate NPS can mask important signals. Break down by: customer size, industry, product line, time as customer, CSM. Where is NPS highest? Lowest? Why?

*Follow-up question is more valuable than score:* "Điều chính nào ảnh hưởng đến điểm số của anh/chị?" — This open-text answer contains actionable insights that the 0-10 number never does.

**NPS là indicator, không là manager:**
High NPS không automatically mean referrals — some Promoters never refer. Low NPS từ một customer doesn't mean they'll churn — some Detractors stay for reasons unrelated to satisfaction. Use NPS as one signal among many.

**NPS → Action workflow:**
- Detractors (0-6): Personal outreach within 24h. "Tôi thấy anh/chị có điểm số thấp — tôi muốn hiểu và address ngay nếu có thể." Recovery opportunity.
- Passives (7-8): Follow-up to understand what's holding them back from 9-10. Usually addressable concerns.
- Promoters (9-10): Ask for referrals, testimonials, case studies. "Rất vui khi anh/chị có experience tốt như vậy — có anh/chị nào trong network có thể benefit tương tự không?"

> **Bài học:** NPS is best run as closed-loop process. Survey → score → personal follow-up based on score segment → action → close loop with customer. Without the human follow-up, NPS is just data with no leverage. With follow-up, NPS becomes relationship management tool.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến:** Running NPS once/year. Annual NPS snapshot tells you how customers feel annually; doesn't catch problems when they're small and fixable. Quarterly NPS of key accounts, annual NPS of full base.

> **Cạm bẫy:** Gaming NPS. Asking for NPS right after a positive moment ("how great are we?") inflates score but doesn't reflect real sentiment. Random timing, or consistent timing tied to natural relationship touchpoints, gives more accurate data.
