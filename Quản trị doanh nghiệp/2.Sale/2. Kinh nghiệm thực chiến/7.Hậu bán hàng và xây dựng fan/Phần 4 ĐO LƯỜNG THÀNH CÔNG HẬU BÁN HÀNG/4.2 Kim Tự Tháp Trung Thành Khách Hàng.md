### Kim Tự Tháp Trung Thành Khách Hàng

Kim Tự Tháp Trung Thành là framework phân loại khách hàng từ thấp nhất (transaction) đến cao nhất (brand ambassador), giúp bạn understand where each customer is và what investment to make to move them up.

**5 tầng của Kim Tự Tháp:**

*Tầng 1 — Transactional Customer (bottom):*
Đã mua một lần. Satisfaction neutral. No strong loyalty. Could easily leave for competitor.
Investment: Product reliability và adequate support.
Goal: → move to Repeat Customer.

*Tầng 2 — Repeat Customer:*
Mua nhiều lần. Ổn định nhưng chưa loyal sâu. Would consider switching if something better comes along.
Investment: Consistency, personalized service, early access to value.
Goal: → move to Loyal Customer.

*Tầng 3 — Loyal Customer:*
Strong preference for you. Wouldn't switch easily. Gives you benefit of the doubt when things go wrong.
Investment: Relationship depth, QBRs, WOW moments.
Goal: → move to Advocate.

*Tầng 4 — Advocate:*
Actively recommends you to others without being asked. Defends you in competitive conversations.
Investment: Recognition, community involvement, special roles.
Goal: → move to Brand Ambassador.

*Tầng 5 — Brand Ambassador (top):*
Volunteer spokesperson. Speaks at events, writes testimonials, proactively champions you. Relationship with your brand is part of their professional identity.
Investment: Deep partnership, co-creation opportunities, public recognition.

**Practical Application:**
Map mỗi customer onto pyramid. Track over time. Identify: (1) Who is at risk of sliding down? (2) Who has potential to move up? (3) What specific investment would move them?

| Customer | Current Tier | Target Tier | Next Action |
|----------|-------------|-------------|-------------|
| Công ty A | Loyal | Advocate | Ask for referral, QBR upcoming |
| Công ty B | Repeat | Loyal | Schedule relationship check-in |
| Công ty C | Transactional | Repeat | Proactive check-in, find upsell need |

> **Bài học:** Audit your customer portfolio with this pyramid this week. How many customers in each tier? Is the distribution healthy (pyramid shape, with some in each tier) or bottom-heavy (mostly transactional)? Distribution tells you health of your long-term revenue pipeline.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến:** Treating all customers with same investment level. Top of pyramid customers deserve more attention và deeper relationship investment. Bottom of pyramid customers need enough to move up, but not same investment as Advocates. Differentiated investment strategy is not unfair — it's strategic.

> **Cạm bẫy:** Assuming pyramid position is permanent. A Brand Ambassador who experiences significant product failure without adequate recovery can drop to Detractor quickly. Monitor for signals of movement in either direction và respond accordingly.
