### Giai Đoạn 3: 30 Ngày Trở Đi — Long-term Relationship

Giai đoạn 30+ ngày là nơi revenue thật được tạo ra: renewals, expansions, upsells, và referrals. Nhưng cũng là nơi relationship thường bị neglected sau initial onboarding excitement fades. Systematic attention in this phase is what separates one-time transactions from lifetime customer relationships.

**Quarterly Business Review (QBR) Framework:**
Mỗi quý, schedule structured review với key stakeholders:
1. *Review results:* Đã achieve gì so với goals được set (từ discovery/onboarding)?
2. *Current state:* Đang gặp challenges gì? Có gì thay đổi trong business context?
3. *Planning ahead:* Mục tiêu tiếp theo của họ là gì? Có nhu cầu mới nào nổi lên?
4. *Feedback:* Điều gì đang work tốt? Điều gì cần cải thiện?

**Expansion Opportunity Identification:**
Trong long-term relationships, expansion opportunities emerge naturally from:
- New pain points revealed in QBRs
- Company growth (new departments, more users)
- New product/feature launches relevant to their needs
- Referral networks they can introduce you to

**Referral Nurturing:**
Tốt nhất để ask for referrals: 30-60 ngày sau khi customer has experienced first significant value (not immediately after signing, not too long after when excitement fades).

Script: "Anh/chị [Tên], tôi rất vui khi thấy [specific result]. Tôi curious — anh/chị có biết người nào khác trong network của mình đang gặp challenge tương tự mà có thể benefit từ conversation với chúng tôi không? Không áp lực — nếu có người phù hợp, một intro email từ anh/chị sẽ rất valuable."

**Longevity touchpoints:**
- *Monthly:* Newsletter hoặc insight relevant tới their industry/role
- *Quarterly:* QBR hoặc structured check-in
- *Annual:* Relationship review — are they getting full value? What's next year look like?
- *Ad-hoc:* Any time you see news about their company, industry, or a result from similar customer — reach out with a brief personal note

> **Bài học:** Build a "customer portfolio calendar" — list all current customers với planned touchpoints cho next 12 months. Seeing it visually reveals where gaps are. A customer you haven't touched in 6 months is a churn risk; a customer you've touched meaningfully every month is a referral opportunity.

> **Phân tích sâu:** Bain & Company research: cost of acquiring new customer is 5-7x cost of retaining existing one. More importantly: customers who have been with company 3+ years spend 67% more on average than new customers. The compounding effect of long-term relationships is one of the most powerful revenue drivers in business — and it starts with systematic 30-day+ relationship investment.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến:** Only reaching out when you have something to sell. Customer can tell when touchpoints are purely commercial. Balance: 70-80% of touchpoints should be value-add (industry insights, relevant content, proactive problem solving), 20-30% can be commercial (renewal reminders, expansion discussions).

> **Cạm bẫy:** QBR hóa thành monologue về your products. QBR is primarily THEIR review — how are THEY doing, what do THEY need, what are THEIR goals next quarter. Your product features are secondary context, not the primary agenda.
