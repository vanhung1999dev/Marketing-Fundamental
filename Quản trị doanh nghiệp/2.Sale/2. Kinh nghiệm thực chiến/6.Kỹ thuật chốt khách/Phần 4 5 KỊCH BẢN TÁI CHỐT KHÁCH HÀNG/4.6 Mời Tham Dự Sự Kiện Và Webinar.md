### Mời Tham Dự Sự Kiện Và Webinar — Re-Engagement Qua Giá Trị

Event invitation là một trong những most natural re-engagement touches — it's not a sales pitch, it's an offer of value. When done right, prospects who declined purchase often attend events, re-engage with your brand, and sometimes convert later.

**Tại sao event invitation effective cho re-engagement:**
- Low commitment: attending event is less commitment than purchase decision
- Neutral ground: event context is educational, not sales-heavy
- Peer connection: they meet others like them using your product/service
- Demonstration of expertise: if your event content is valuable, perceived expertise increases

**Types of events valuable for re-engagement:**
- Webinars with high-value content relevant to their industry pain points
- Industry roundtables or peer learning sessions
- Product demo days (especially with new features)
- Customer success stories panels
- Virtual coffee chats with industry experts

**Template event invitation to cold prospect:**
```
"Anh/chị [Tên],

Chúng ta đã nói chuyện về [subject] khoảng [time] trước.

Tôi muốn invite anh/chị đến [event name] vào [date] —
một [webinar/roundtable] về [topic cực kỳ relevant với pain họ mentioned].

Speaker sẽ include [credible speaker names/titles].
Nội dung sẽ cover [2-3 specific takeaways relevant to their pain].

Đây là event hữu ích bất kể anh/chị có work với chúng tôi hay không —
vì [content có standalone value].

Link đăng ký: [link]
Hoàn toàn free. Không có sales pitch.

Rất welcome nếu anh/chị có thể join!"
```

**Sau event — natural follow-up:**
"Anh/chị [Tên], cảm ơn anh/chị đã join session hôm qua. Điều gì anh/chị thấy helpful nhất?" — opens conversation naturally without pressure.

> **Bài học:** Quarterly event strategy: organize or curate 1-2 valuable events per quarter, invite all warm và closed-lost prospects. Measure: how many re-engage? How many eventually convert? Over time, this becomes powerful pipeline-regeneration engine.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến:** "Free webinar" mà thực ra là 60-minute product pitch. Attendees feel deceived và trust decreases — exact opposite of goal. Keep events genuinely valuable và educational. Product information can be 10% of a webinar, not 80%.

> **Cạm bẫy:** Following up invitation with immediate sales pitch. Give people time to digest the event content. Wait 3-5 days post-event before reaching out with softer check-in.
