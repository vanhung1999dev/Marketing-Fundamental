### Im Lặng Chiến Lược — Silence Close

Silence Close là kỹ thuật đơn giản nhất và hardest to master: sau khi ask for commitment — shut up. Don't add more, don't explain more, don't give them outs. Just ask and wait. Người đầu tiên nói sau moment này thường là người nhượng bộ.

**Cơ chế tâm lý:** Silence creates discomfort. Trong discomfort đó, người ta feel compelled to fill the space. Khi bạn đã asked for commitment và stop talking — pressure self-generates. Buyer starts thinking through pros and cons, often talks themselves into the decision or reveals the real remaining concern.

**Thực hiện Silence Close:**
```
[Sau khi đã present, handled objections, established value]

"Anh/chị [Tên], tôi nghĩ chúng ta đã cover đủ để anh/chị
có thể ra quyết định với confidence. Đề xuất của tôi là
[specific option/package/next step].

Anh/chị cảm thấy thế nào?"

[Stop talking. Look at them expectantly. Wait.]
```

**Khoảng im lặng thích hợp:** 5-10 giây cảm thấy như mãi mãi, nhưng thực ra rất short. Resist urge để fill silence sau 3 giây. Let it run.

**Điều thường xảy ra trong silence:**
- Buyer says yes → great
- Buyer says "Okay, tôi sẽ làm" → done
- Buyer reveals concern: "Tôi chỉ cần hỏi..." → now you know what's blocking

**Khi im lặng bị phá vỡ sai:** Bạn nói: "Hoặc nếu anh/chị chưa sẵn sàng, tôi có thể..." → bạn vừa give them the out. Đừng làm vậy. Wait.

> **Bài học:** Practice silence in low-stakes settings first — meetings with colleagues, phone calls. Get comfortable with 5-10 seconds of silence without filling it. Discomfort with silence is one of the most costly habits in sales.

> **Phân tích sâu:** Research on negotiation: those who speak first after a proposal typically accept less favorable terms. The silence dynamic is powerful enough that experienced negotiators train specifically on "strategic silence." In sales context, the same applies: premature talking after asking for commitment often results in unnecessary concessions (discounts, added features) that weren't needed — because buyer was about to say yes.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến:** Break silence with nervousness babble: "Và cũng anh/chị ơi, tôi quên mention rằng chúng tôi cũng có thể..." — adding features or information that wasn't needed. This signals nervousness and often triggers buyer to re-evaluate rather than commit.

> **Cạm bẫy:** Over-use Silence Close — using it in early conversations before enough trust and value is established. Silence works as close technique when buyer is ready; used too early it feels awkward and creates distance.
