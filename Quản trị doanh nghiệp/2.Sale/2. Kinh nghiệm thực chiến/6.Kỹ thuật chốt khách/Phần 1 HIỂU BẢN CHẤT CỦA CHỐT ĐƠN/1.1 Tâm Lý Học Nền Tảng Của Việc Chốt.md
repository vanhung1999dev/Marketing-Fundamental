### Tâm Lý Học Nền Tảng Của Việc Chốt

Closing không phải là pha cuối của sales — nó là logical conclusion của một process đúng. Nếu discovery tốt, presentation tốt, objections được addressed — closing là natural next step. Khi closing feels hard, it's usually a signal something earlier in the process was incomplete. "Can't close" thường nghĩa "didn't discover deeply enough" hoặc "didn't establish enough value."

**Tại sao người ta không mua dù muốn mua:** Loss aversion. Mọi purchase decision involve perceived risk: financial risk ("what if I waste money?"), performance risk ("what if it doesn't work?"), social risk ("what if my boss thinks I chose wrong?"), psychological risk ("what if I regret this?"). People who want to buy are simultaneously afraid of regretting buying. Closing well means reducing these risks, not overcoming buyer resistance.

**Hai mode của closing:**

*Mode 1 — Decision Facilitation (Tốt):* Giúp người đã muốn mua overcome internal hesitation. "Tôi thấy anh/chị đã understand value và situation đã clear — điều gì anh/chị còn cần để comfortable với decision này?" → Low pressure, high support.

*Mode 2 — Pressure Closing (Xấu):* Thuyết phục người chưa convinced hoặc không phù hợp phải mua. Tactics kiểu này may win short-term deal but creates: buyer's remorse, high churn, negative referrals, damaged reputation. Not sustainable.

**Psychological readiness indicators của buyer:**
- "Khi tôi dùng..." (thay vì "nếu") — future pacing, đã mentally committed
- Câu hỏi về logistics: "Implementation mất bao lâu?", "Setup như thế nào?"
- Hỏi về references: "Anh/chị có thể cho tôi nói chuyện với customer nào không?"
- Tone thay đổi: Từ evaluative sang collaborative
- Body language/energy shift: More engaged, leaning in (in person)

Khi thấy 2+ signals này → time to close. Continue pitching = risk over-talking past yes.

> **Bài học:** Trong 10 deals tiếp theo, note specifically: "Điều gì làm tôi biết đây là đúng thời điểm để close?" Build your personal pattern recognition for readiness signals based on your specific product và customer type.

> **Phân tích sâu:** Research của Cialdini's "Pre-Suasion" (2016): the state of mind immediately before a request matters as much as the request itself. People in state of curiosity say yes more. People just told about someone with similar situation who succeeded say yes more. This means closing is not just about WHAT you ask — it's about the state you help buyer be in when you ask. Create the right state (safe, informed, peer-validated) before the ask.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến:** Treating closing as separate technique from the rest of the process. "Tôi đã pitch tốt rồi, giờ cần technique để chốt." Wrong mental model. Best closing is invisible — it happens naturally when rest of process is excellent.

> **Cạm bẫy:** Waiting for perfect moment that never comes. Some buyers need to be guided to the decision; they won't volunteer "OK I'll buy now." Asking for commitment at appropriate readiness level is not pushy — it's helpful. Not asking is leaving them to decide alone with no support.
