### Phân Loại Khách Hàng (Lead Qualification)

Lead qualification — xác định prospect nào xứng đáng đầu tư thời gian sales — là một trong những skills quan trọng nhất và underrated nhất trong bán hàng. Mọi salesperson có hữu hạn thời gian; cách allocate time đó giữa prospects có quality khác nhau quyết định phần lớn outcome. Người qualify tốt không nhất thiết work harder — họ work smarter bằng cách không waste time on wrong opportunities.

**BANT và Modern Alternatives**: BANT (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline) là classic qualification framework nhưng có limitations — đặc biệt early in cycle khi prospects chưa có defined budget hoặc timeline. Modern alternatives được nhiều enterprise teams sử dụng: *MEDDIC* (Metrics/Economic Buyer/Decision Criteria/Decision Process/Identify Pain/Champion) — comprehensive nhưng complex; *SPIN* (Situation/Problem/Implication/Need-payoff) — questioning framework; *ANUM* (Authority/Need/Urgency/Money) — variation của BANT với priority shift. Quan trọng: dùng framework làm guide, không interrogation checklist.

**Qualification as Conversation**: qualification không phải interrogate prospect để extract information — đó là conversation để understand their situation và mutually determine if there's a fit. Frame qualification questions as genuine curiosity: "Giúp tôi hiểu, hiện tại anh/chị xử lý [problem] như thế nào?" rather than "Ngân sách của anh/chị là bao nhiêu?" The former builds rapport và yields richer information; the latter feels transactional and puts prospect on defensive.

**Disqualification as Skill**: equally important to qualifying in is qualifying out — recognizing when a prospect is not a good fit và ending the sales process professionally. Disqualification saves both parties time và preserves relationship for future. Signs to disqualify: no budget trong foreseeable future; decision-maker is not engaged và unreachable; problem isn't significant enough to justify investment; requirements fundamentally don't match capability; no timeline for decision. The best salespeople have high disqualification rates early in cycle — they invest heavily in opportunities with genuine fit.

**Lead Scoring Models**: trong organizations với high lead volume, lead scoring — assigning numerical scores to leads based on fit và engagement signals — helps prioritize outreach. Fit score: company size, industry, technology, geography matches ICP. Engagement score: website visits, content downloads, email opens, demo requests. High fit + high engagement = hot lead; low fit + high engagement = interested but wrong company; high fit + low engagement = right company but not yet aware. Score combines both dimensions.

> **Bài học:** Sau khi close deal hoặc lose deal, look back và identify: "Ở đâu trong process chúng ta biết — hoặc should have known — rằng deal này không qualify?" Retrospective qualification analysis reveals common patterns của unqualified deals that consumed significant sales time. Use these patterns to tighten early qualification criteria và catch these deals earlier in next cycle.

> **Phân tích sâu:** Forrester research shows: sales reps spend only 33% of their time actually selling — the rest is administrative work, internal meetings, và working unqualified opportunities. Better qualification — spending less time on deals that won't close — is one of the highest-leverage productivity improvements available without increasing headcount. A 20% improvement in qualification effectiveness can yield 30-40% more time on viable deals.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến #1:** Qualifying too quickly với surface-level information. "They have budget và need" isn't sufficient. Who exactly is the decision-maker? What's their decision process? Do they have an incumbent vendor? What's the consequence of not changing? Shallow qualification leads to deals getting stuck or lost later when these gaps surface. Front-load qualification effort.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến #2:** Over-qualifying to the point of disqualifying good opportunities prematurely. Some of the best deals start without clear budget or defined timeline — the salesperson's job is to help create the urgency và business case. Don't disqualify opportunities simply because the prospect hasn't done the buying homework yet; differentiate between "not qualified yet" và "fundamentally not a fit."

> **Cạm bẫy:** Qualification criteria that exist on paper but aren't enforced in pipeline reviews. If manager accepts any opportunity into pipeline regardless of qualification, salespeople stop qualifying seriously because there's no consequence. Pipeline review rigor — asking hard qualification questions about every deal — reinforces that qualification standards matter.
