### Tìm Kiếm Khách Hàng Tiềm Năng (Prospecting)

Prospecting — tìm kiếm và tiếp cận khách hàng tiềm năng — là đầu vào của toàn bộ sales engine. Không có pipeline, không có sales. Không có prospecting tốt, pipeline đầy nhưng chất lượng kém. Nhiều salesperson thành công ở việc closing nhưng struggle vì không có đủ prospects; nhiều salesperson có đầy pipeline nhưng waste time với unqualified opportunities. Prospecting hiệu quả giải quyết cả hai vấn đề.

**ICP-Driven Prospecting**: bắt đầu bằng Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) — mô tả chi tiết công ty và decision-maker phù hợp nhất với giải pháp của bạn. ICP thường bao gồm: industry/vertical; company size (revenue, headcount); growth stage (startup vs. mid-market vs. enterprise); technology stack (nếu solution có integration requirements); geographic focus; business model; và pain profile (problems they likely have). Prospecting tanpa ICP là spray-and-pray — tốn thời gian, tỷ lệ thấp, và demoralize team.

**Multi-Channel Prospecting**: không có single channel đủ effective để rely on exclusively. Modern prospecting mix thường bao gồm: *Outbound email sequences* — personalized, multi-touch (3-7 emails over 2-3 weeks); *Cold calling* — still effective khi done well, especially combined with email; *LinkedIn outreach* — warm, social-proof enabled; *Referrals từ existing customers* — highest conversion rate, lowest cost; *Content-driven inbound* — positioning dẫn đến prospects reaching out. Tỷ lệ mix depends on market, solution, và ICP but diversity of channels reduces single-channel risk.

**Personalization at Scale**: personalization tăng response rates dramatically nhưng không thể personalize every outreach manually at scale. Framework: *Tier 1 (dream accounts)* — fully researched, highly personalized; *Tier 2 (qualified accounts)* — account-level personalization (industry pain, recent news, known tech stack); *Tier 3 (broad reach)* — persona-level personalization (relevant for their role, not specific company). Invest personalization effort proportional to account strategic value.

**Prospecting Cadence và Consistency**: prospecting is a discipline, not an event. Most reps fail at prospecting not because they lack skill but because they do it inconsistently — prospecting heavily when pipeline is low, stopping when it's full. Quota-carrying reps should block dedicated prospecting time daily/weekly regardless of current pipeline level. "Full pipeline today" doesn't mean "closed deals next month" — pipeline needs constant replenishment.

> **Bài học:** Track prospecting activities (calls made, emails sent, LinkedIn messages, responses, meetings booked) separately from pipeline metrics. Activity metrics lag into pipeline metrics lag into revenue metrics — understanding this cascade allows early intervention. If prospecting activities are low this week, pipeline will be thin in 4 weeks, và revenue will be short in 8-12 weeks. Leading indicators give time to course-correct.

> **Phân tích sâu:** RAIN Group research on top-performing salespeople reveals: họ prospect 39% more hours per week than average performers — not because they work more total hours, but because they protect prospecting time from administrative và reactive work. They also have 52% more scheduled meetings per week. The compounding advantage of consistent prospecting over time is dramatic: a rep who prospects 20% more consistently outperforms peers not by 20% but by significantly more due to pipeline health effects.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến #1:** Treating prospecting as punishment — "something I have to do when pipeline is low" — rather than professional discipline. Reps with this mindset do it reluctantly and inconsistently. The mindset shift: prospecting is the most important activity in sales because everything else depends on it. Pipeline generation is both the hardest and highest-leverage activity.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến #2:** Giving up too early. Research shows most sales require 5+ touches before a response, but many reps give up after 2. Multi-touch, multi-channel sequences that persist respectfully over 2-3 weeks dramatically outperform single-touch outreach. Persistence, within professional boundaries, is a prospecting superpower.

> **Cạm bẫy:** Confusing activity with effectiveness. High call volume với irrelevant messaging to wrong ICP is worse than lower volume với highly targeted, relevant outreach. Measure both activities (leading) và conversion rates (effectiveness): calls → connections, connections → meetings booked, meetings → qualified opportunities. Low conversion at any transition point signals messaging hoặc targeting problem, not effort problem.
