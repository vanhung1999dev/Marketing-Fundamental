### Kiểm Soát Chất Lượng Quy Trình

Kiểm soát chất lượng quy trình bán hàng (Sales Process Quality Control) là hệ thống các checks, standards, và reviews đảm bảo rằng khi process được executed, nó được executed tốt — không phải chỉ completed. Completing a checklist là khác với completing it with quality. Một rep có thể "send proposal" nhưng gửi proposal generic không address customer's specific needs; một rep có thể "log discovery meeting" nhưng meeting không actually discover key information. Quality control closes this gap.

**Execution Standards per Stage**: mỗi stage trong sales process phải có execution standards — không phải chỉ "did it happen" mà "what does good look like?". Discovery standard: "rep can articulate customer's top 3 pain points, business impact of those pains, và who is affected" — not just "meeting occurred." Proposal standard: "proposal references specific pain points from discovery, includes quantified ROI, names the economic buyer as addressee" — not just "proposal sent." Document these standards in playbook và use them as review criteria in deal reviews.

**Call và Email Quality Review**: regular review của actual customer interactions — call recordings (với consent), email content — provides ground truth về execution quality. Listening to calls reveals: does rep actually ask discovery questions or just pitch? Do they handle objections gracefully or capitulate? Do they listen effectively or interrupt? Email review reveals: are emails personalized or templated? Clear next step stated? Appropriate length? Many organizations use AI-assisted tools (Gong, Chorus) to analyze patterns across all calls automatically.

**Deal Review as Quality Checkpoint**: structured deal reviews — where manager reviews specific opportunities with rep using consistent criteria — serve quality control function at deal level. Review criteria tied to execution standards: "Show me the discovery notes — can you articulate their top pain and business impact?" "Who is your champion, and how do you know they're actually advocating for you internally?" These questions catch quality issues at deal level before they become win/loss outcomes at quarter level.

**Continuous Calibration**: quality standards must be regularly calibrated across team to ensure everyone has same understanding của "good." When one manager has higher standard for "qualified opportunity" than another, pipeline quality is inconsistent across regions. Hold quarterly calibration sessions where examples of specific deals are reviewed together: "Is this a qualified opportunity or not? Here's why I think yes/no." Calibration creates shared standard.

> **Bài học:** Create a "Quality Checklist" for each major stage — 5-7 yes/no questions that define whether stage was completed with quality. Rep self-reviews against checklist before advancing deal; manager reviews checklist in pipeline review. Over time, track which checklist items correlate most strongly với win rate — this reveals which quality standards matter most và should be emphasized.

> **Phân tích sâu:** Challenger Sale research (Dixon và Adamson) found: the biggest differentiator between average và top-performing reps isn't what they do — it's how they do it. Specifically, top performers are more likely to bring commercial insight (teach the customer something new about their situation), tailor their message to specific stakeholder concerns, và take control of the sale constructively rather than adapting passively to customer lead. These are quality of execution dimensions, not activity dimensions.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến #1:** Measuring activity completion rather than activity quality. "Rep had 10 discovery calls this week" — great, but what was the quality? Did they actually discover relevant information that enables good proposals? Completion metrics are easier to measure but quality metrics predict outcomes better. Invest in measuring quality even though it requires more judgment.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến #2:** One-size-fits-all quality standards for reps at different experience levels. Junior reps need more prescriptive standards (specific questions to ask, exact email formats to use); senior reps need framework guidelines but more latitude for judgment. Over-prescribing for senior reps stifles effectiveness; under-prescribing for junior reps enables inconsistency and errors.

> **Cạm bẫy:** Confusing CRM data entry completeness với execution quality. A fully filled-out CRM record doesn't mean quality execution occurred — it means someone filled out fields. A deal review conversation that probes understanding and strategy execution is more revealing of quality than field completion rate. Don't let "CRM compliance" become proxy for "execution quality."
