### Đánh Giá Hiệu Suất Kinh Doanh

Đánh giá performance một cách công bằng, comprehensive, và actionable — không phải chỉ "anh/chị có hit quota không?" — là nền tảng của continuous improvement và talent development. Performance assessment tốt identifies what's working, what's not, và specifically what needs to change.

**Performance Assessment Framework**: đánh giá theo 3 dimensions: (1) *Outcomes* — kết quả đạt được: revenue, quota attainment, new logos, expansion; (2) *Activities/Process* — behaviors leading to outcomes: call volume, proposal quality, deal velocity, customer interactions; (3) *Competencies* — skills và behaviors: product knowledge, relationship management, negotiation, strategic thinking. Người có good outcomes nhưng poor process là "lucky" và inconsistent; person với good process nhưng poor outcomes là "unlucky" hoặc có resource/territory issues.

**Benchmarking Performance**: numbers without context are meaningless. Benchmark against: (1) *Self* — is this person improving over time? (2) *Peer group* — vs comparable reps với similar territories và tenure; (3) *Target* — vs quota/budget; (4) *Market* — vs industry benchmark win rates, cycle times. Each benchmark provides different insight.

**Quota Attainment Distribution Analysis**: healthy sales organization has 60-70% of reps hitting quota. If >90% hit — quota may be too low; if <50% hit — quota too aggressive hoặc there are systemic issues (bad product fit, territory imbalance, hiring problem). Distribution also matters: ideal is tight bell curve; wide distribution signals inconsistent coaching or territory inequality.

**Performance Conversation Excellence**: đánh giá is conversation, not declaration. Structure: (1) *Rep self-assessment* — "How do you see your performance this period? What's working? What's not?"; (2) *Data sharing* — "Here's what I'm seeing in the numbers..."; (3) *Root cause analysis* — "What do you think is driving the gap/success?"; (4) *Forward planning* — "What will you do differently? What support do you need?" Great performance conversations end với clear, specific commitments, not vague improvement goals.

> **Bài học:** Implement "Weekly Scorecard Review" với each rep: 5-minute check-in focused on 3-5 leading indicators (not just revenue). "Anh có bao nhiêu first meetings tuần này? Pipeline mới add? Số proposals out?" Leading indicator review allows coaching while there's still time to change outcomes — not just post-mortem after quarter ends.

> **Phân tích sâu:** Research on performance management by Marcus Buckingham ("Nine Lies About Work") suggests: performance rating systems that force distribution (ranking people 1-10) destroy engagement và are statistically unreliable. Better approach is frequent, specific, forward-looking check-ins focused on "what do you need to do your best work?" rather than backward-looking ratings. Annual reviews have minimal behavior change impact; weekly coaching has maximum.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến #1:** Confuse performance with potential. High-performer today may have low potential for growth; struggling performer may have high ceiling if given right development. Assessment systems that only measure current performance miss development opportunity với high-potential people.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến #2:** Attribute all performance to individual without accounting for context. Rep in shrinking territory with competitive disadvantage may underperform not due to capability but due to context. Fair performance assessment accounts for controllable factors vs uncontrollable factors.

> **Cạm bẫy:** Recency bias — evaluating full period performance based primarily on most recent weeks. Memory is biased toward recent events; a great quarter with one bad month at end gets assessed more negatively than it deserves. Systematic data review across full period prevents recency distortion.
