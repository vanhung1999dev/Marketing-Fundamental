### Đánh Giá Chất Lượng Dữ Liệu

Biết cách đánh giá độ tin cậy, tính đầy đủ, và tính nhất quán của dữ liệu trước khi sử dụng để ra quyết định là kỹ năng bảo vệ tổ chức khỏi costly mistakes dựa trên flawed data. "Trust but verify" là principle cần apply với mọi dataset — kể cả những data từ hệ thống bạn đã dùng lâu.

**Data Quality Dimensions**: (1) *Accuracy* — data có phản ánh đúng thực tế không? (CRM win rate có match actual revenue?); (2) *Completeness* — có missing values không? Bao nhiêu % records complete? (3) *Consistency* — cùng một metric có được define và tracked nhất quán không? (deal là "won" khi nào?); (4) *Timeliness* — data có current không? Outdated data for time-sensitive decisions là problem; (5) *Uniqueness* — có duplicate records không? (same customer counted twice?); (6) *Validity* — data có trong expected range không? (close date before deal open date là invalid).

**Data Quality Assessment Process**: trước khi using data for important decisions: (1) *Spot check* — manually verify sample of records against source; (2) *Distribution check* — are numbers in expected range? Any impossible values?; (3) *Completeness check* — what % of records have all critical fields populated?; (4) *Consistency check* — same numbers across systems? (CRM vs finance vs operations?); (5) *Historical accuracy check* — did past forecasts based on this data prove accurate?

**Common Data Quality Issues trong Sales**: missing close dates (30%+ of deals in many CRMs); inconsistent deal stage definitions across reps; revenue numbers in CRM not matching finance system due to different timing conventions; customer counts including churned customers; activity data inflated by auto-logging without rep review.

**Communicating Data Uncertainty**: khi presenting analysis based on imperfect data, communicate uncertainty explicitly: "Đây là best estimate dựa trên available data — confidence level moderate vì [specific gap]. Khuyến nghị của tôi là [X] với caveat rằng [Y] cần verification." Được ưu tiên trên false precision hoặc withholding uncertain analysis.

> **Bài học:** Trước bất kỳ major decision nào được inform bởi data analysis, hỏi: "What would have to be wrong with our data for this conclusion to change?" Nếu câu trả lời là "nhiều thứ có thể sai," cần data validation trước khi proceed. Nếu conclusion is robust even with data imperfections, proceed with clearly stated assumptions.

> **Phân tích sâu:** Theo MIT Sloan research on analytics maturity, organizations với strong data governance practices make significantly better decisions at significantly lower cost than organizations without — not because they have more data, but because they have higher confidence in the data they have. Investment in data quality is consistently undervalued relative to its decision-making impact.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến #1:** Using data without questioning its provenance. "The system shows X" is often treated as fact. But systems only show what's been entered; people enter data incompletely, inconsistently, và sometimes incorrectly. Healthy skepticism về data source và collection process is necessary.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến #2:** Fixing data quality problems by cleaning the data once rather than fixing the collection process. If data entry is inconsistent, cleaning the historical data once doesn't solve future data quality issues. Root cause fix: improve collection process, training, và validation rules at point of entry.

> **Cạm bẫy:** Perfect data paralysis — refusing to make decisions until data quality is perfect. Perfect data is unattainable; waiting for it means never deciding. The right standard is "good enough for the decision at hand, with understood limitations." High-stakes decisions warrant higher data quality bar than low-stakes ones.
