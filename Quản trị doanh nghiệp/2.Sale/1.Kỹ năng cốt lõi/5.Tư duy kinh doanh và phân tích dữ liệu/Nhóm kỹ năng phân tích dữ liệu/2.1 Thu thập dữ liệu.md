### Thu Thập Dữ Liệu Kinh Doanh

Xây dựng hệ thống thu thập dữ liệu đúng loại, đúng chất lượng, và đúng tần suất là bước đầu tiên của data-driven decision making — và bước này thường bị underinvested so với analysis. "Garbage in, garbage out" — phân tích dữ liệu kém chất lượng không chỉ vô dụng mà còn dangerous vì nó tạo ra false confidence.

**Data Taxonomy cho Sales Organizations**: phân loại data theo nguồn: (1) *CRM data* — deal stages, activity logs, win/loss, close rates, cycle time; (2) *Financial data* — revenue by segment, product, channel; gross margin; CAC; (3) *Customer data* — satisfaction scores, usage data, support tickets, churn signals; (4) *Market data* — competitive intelligence, market size estimates, industry benchmarks; (5) *Activity data* — calls made, emails sent, meetings held, proposals submitted. Biết bạn đang có data nào và thiếu data nào là starting point cho data strategy.

**Data Collection Best Practices**: (1) *Collect at point of activity* — nhập data vào CRM ngay sau cuộc gọi, không để đến cuối tuần (memory degradation); (2) *Standardize fields* — dropdown menus và structured fields thay vì free text cho searchable, analyzable data; (3) *Define và train on definitions* — "qualified lead" có nghĩa là gì cụ thể? Mọi người cần dùng definition nhất quán; (4) *Automation first* — nếu data có thể được captured automatically (email tracking, calendar sync), không cần manual input.

**Qualitative vs Quantitative Data Balance**: quantitative data (numbers, percentages) tells you *what*; qualitative data (interviews, open-ended feedback) tells you *why*. Both are essential. Many organizations over-index on quantitative (easy to collect và analyze) và under-index on qualitative (harder but more insightful). Monthly customer interviews give qualitative insight that no dashboard can replicate.

**Data Governance Basics**: ai có thể add/edit/delete which data? What is source of truth when systems conflict? How long is data retained? Who is responsible for data quality? These governance questions sound bureaucratic but prevent the data chaos that renders data useless. "Which number should I use?" là signal of absent data governance.

> **Bài học:** Làm "Data Audit" quarterly: list top 10 business questions bạn cần answered to make decisions. Then check: do you have data to answer each? Is that data clean and accessible? Gaps in this audit become data collection priorities — collect what you need to answer your most important questions, not collect everything possible.

> **Phân tích sâu:** Theo 2023 Salesforce State of Sales report, sales reps spend only 28% of time actually selling — rest is on administrative tasks, many of which involve data entry. Companies that invest in sales automation và CRM hygiene see significant productivity gains không chỉ vì time saved on data entry mà vì better data quality enables better decisions. Investment in data infrastructure is investment in decision quality.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến #1:** Collecting data without knowing what decisions it will inform. "More data is always better" is false when it creates data overload và analysis paralysis. Biết specific decisions bạn need to make → identify data needed → collect that data. Purposeful data collection is better than comprehensive data collection.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến #2:** Trust in data quality that hasn't been validated. "Our CRM shows 45% win rate" — has that win rate definition been consistently applied? Are lost deals being logged? Is it wins against all opportunities or only late-stage ones? Question data quality before drawing conclusions from it.

> **Cạm bẫy:** Data hoarding từ multiple tools mà không integration plan. Companies often have data in CRM, marketing automation, financial system, customer success tool, và support platform — all separate, all inconsistent. Data without integration strategy creates silos, not insights. Integration strategy (even simple: one single source of truth dashboard) must accompany tool procurement.
