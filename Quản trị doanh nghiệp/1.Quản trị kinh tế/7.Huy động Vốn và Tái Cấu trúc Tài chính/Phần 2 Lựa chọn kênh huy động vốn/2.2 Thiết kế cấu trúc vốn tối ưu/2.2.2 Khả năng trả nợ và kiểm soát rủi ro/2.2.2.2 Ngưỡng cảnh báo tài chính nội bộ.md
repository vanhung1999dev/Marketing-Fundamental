### Ngưỡng Cảnh Báo Tài Chính Nội Bộ

Hệ thống ngưỡng cảnh báo nội bộ (internal financial tripwires) là cơ chế phòng ngừa sớm cho tình trạng tài chính xấu đi. Khác với ngưỡng covenant của ngân hàng (vi phạm = đã muộn), ngưỡng nội bộ được thiết lập ở mức cảnh báo trước — để ban lãnh đạo có thời gian hành động trước khi vi phạm nghĩa vụ pháp lý.

> **Bài học:** Tài chính không có "đột nhiên khủng hoảng" — chỉ có "cảnh báo bị bỏ qua." Mọi cuộc khủng hoảng tài chính của doanh nghiệp đều có các dấu hiệu tiền triệu trước 3–6 tháng. Hệ thống cảnh báo sớm giúp ban lãnh đạo nhìn thấy những dấu hiệu này và hành động khi vẫn còn lựa chọn.

---

**Financial Tripwire System — 3 Màu Cảnh Báo:**

```
INTERNAL THRESHOLD SYSTEM (so sánh với bank covenants):

Metric              | Green (Healthy) | Yellow (Monitor) | Red (Action Required)
--------------------|-----------------|------------------|----------------------
DSCR               | ≥ 1.5×          | 1.25–1.5×        | < 1.25×
Net Debt/EBITDA    | ≤ 2.5×          | 2.5–3.5×         | > 3.5×
Current Ratio      | ≥ 1.5×          | 1.0–1.5×         | < 1.0×
ICR (Interest Cover)| ≥ 3.5×         | 2.5–3.5×         | < 2.5×
Cash Coverage Days | ≥ 45 days       | 20–45 days       | < 20 days
D/E Ratio          | ≤ 1.5×          | 1.5–2.5×         | > 2.5×
AR Overdue >90d   | < 5% of AR      | 5–15% of AR      | > 15% of AR
Revenue vs Budget  | ≥ 95%           | 85–95%           | < 85%

BANK COVENANT THRESHOLDS (example):
  DSCR covenant: ≥ 1.2× → Internal tripwire at 1.25× (5% buffer to covenant)
  D/E covenant: ≤ 3.0× → Internal tripwire at 2.5× (17% buffer)
  
RULE: Internal tripwires should be set 15–20% more conservative than bank covenants
      If tripwire triggered: Take action → By the time you reach covenant = already addressed
```

---

**Escalation Protocol Khi Kích Hoạt Cảnh Báo:**

```
WHEN YELLOW THRESHOLD BREACHED:

Within 1 week:
  □ CFO prepares analysis: Which metric? How much? Why?
  □ Root cause: Revenue miss? Cost overrun? Capex overrun? AR delay?
  □ Trajectory: Is this a one-off blip or worsening trend?
  □ Forward projection: At current trajectory, when does Green return?
  
Within 2 weeks:
  □ CFO + CEO review
  □ Action plan: 2–3 concrete steps to return to Green
    (Could be: accelerate AR collection; delay discretionary capex; 
     draw credit line earlier; renegotiate supplier terms)
  □ Timeline and accountabilities assigned
  □ Bank relationship management: Proactive update to relationship manager (optional but smart)

WHEN RED THRESHOLD BREACHED:

Immediate (within 24 hours):
  □ Emergency CFO/CEO meeting
  □ Assess: Is this one metric or multiple?
  □ Cash runway calculation: How many weeks of cash do we have?
  
Within 48 hours:
  □ Board/owner notification
  □ Legal assessment: Are we in covenant breach? (Check loan agreements)
  □ Immediate cash preservation: Freeze discretionary spending
  □ Revenue acceleration: What can we do to accelerate collections?
  □ Debt management: Call relationship manager; brief proactively (don't wait for bank to discover)
  
Within 1 week:
  □ Formal recovery plan: 90-day action plan with weekly milestones
  □ Capital raise assessment: Is equity injection needed? Debt restructuring?
  □ Professional advisor: Engage financial restructuring advisor if appropriate
  □ Scenario planning: Best/base/worst case; identify decision points
```

**Covenant Management — Quản Lý Giao Ước Ngân Hàng:**

```
LOAN COVENANT TRACKER:

Loan: [Bank Name] | Amount: ___ tỷ | Maturity: [Date]

Covenant              | Threshold | Measured | Current Value | Headroom | Status
----------------------|-----------|----------|---------------|----------|-------
DSCR                  | ≥ 1.2×    | Annual   | 1.45×         | +0.25×   | GREEN
Net Debt/EBITDA       | ≤ 3.5×    | Annual   | 2.8×          | +0.7×    | GREEN
D/E ratio             | ≤ 3.0×    | Annual   | 1.8×          | +1.2×    | GREEN
Current ratio         | ≥ 1.0×    | Annual   | 1.3×          | +0.3×    | GREEN

Additional covenant:
  No additional debt > 20 tỷ without consent: [Compliant]
  No dividend when DSCR < 1.3×: [Compliant; DSCR 1.45× ≥ threshold]
  Annual audited accounts submitted within 120 days: [Due: March 31; submitted: Feb 15 ✓]

COVENANT BREACH PROTOCOL:
  Step 1: Immediately review loan agreement for formal cure period (typically 30–60 days)
  Step 2: Notify relationship manager proactively (don't wait for bank's discovery)
  Step 3: Prepare waiver request letter with explanation and remediation plan
  Step 4: If waiver requested: bank will typically grant if: (a) one-time event; 
          (b) management has credibility; (c) business fundamentals are sound
  Step 5: Never ignore a covenant breach — silence = default
```

> **Phân tích sâu:** The most important feature of an internal tripwire system is the escalation protocol — what specifically happens when a threshold is breached. Without predefined escalation, a yellow threshold trigger may be informally noted and forgotten. With predefined escalation (specific actions, specific responsible people, specific timelines), the system has teeth. The difference between companies that navigate financial stress successfully and those that don't is often just this: the former have pre-planned response protocols; the latter improvise under pressure.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến #1:** Set internal thresholds identical to bank covenants (no buffer). This means by the time the internal alarm goes off, you're already at risk of covenant breach. Always build 15–25% buffer between your internal Yellow and the bank's covenant line.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến #2:** Build dashboards that are too complex to maintain monthly. A 50-metric dashboard reviewed annually is useless. A 10-metric scorecard reviewed monthly by CFO and quarterly by Board is highly effective. Simplicity and consistency of review beat completeness.

> **Cạm bẫy:** Assume that proactive communication with the bank about a developing issue is a bad idea (will "scare them"). The opposite is true. Banks are far more comfortable with a borrower who says "We're experiencing a temporary issue; here's our analysis; here's our plan" than one who goes silent and lets the covenant breach be discovered in the annual filing. Relationship managers are people; they remember who was transparent with them.
