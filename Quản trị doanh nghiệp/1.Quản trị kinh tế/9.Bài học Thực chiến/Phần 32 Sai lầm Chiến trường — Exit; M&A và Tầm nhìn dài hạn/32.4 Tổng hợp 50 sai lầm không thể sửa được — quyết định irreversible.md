### Tổng Hợp 50 Sai Lầm Không Thể Sửa Được — Quyết Định Irreversible

Hầu hết sai lầm trong business có thể học từ và correct. Nhưng có một loại sai lầm đặc biệt: Quyết định irreversible — khi bạn thực hiện rồi; bạn sống với consequences. Không phải mọi bad decision đều irreversible; nhưng những cái trong danh sách này: Mỗi cái cost từ vài tháng đến vài năm của life và resources. Biết trước → Slow down on these; think twice; think again.

> **Bài học:** Phần lớn các sai lầm trong danh sách này đều có một điểm chung: Họ được thực hiện quá nhanh; dưới áp lực; hoặc không có đủ thông tin. Rule: Tốc độ của quyết định phải tỷ lệ nghịch với mức độ irreversibility. Càng khó hoàn tác → Càng phải slow down.

---

**Category 1 — Co-founder và Partner:**

```
#1 — Chọn co-founder sai:
  Mức độ không thể hoàn tác: High. Removing co-founder is traumatic; expensive; often company-killing.
  Lỗi thường gặp: Choose friend/family for wrong reasons; not the right fit.
  Phòng ngừa: Date before marriage. Trial period of working together on side project.

#2 — Equity split quá sớm hoặc quá bằng nhau:
  50/50 split: Sounds fair; creates impasse when decisions diverge.
  Fixed split without vesting: Give away equity without performance protection.
  Phòng ngừa: Cliff + vesting schedule on ALL founder equity; never skip.

#3 — Không có shareholder agreement trước khi bắt đầu:
  When things go wrong; no framework → Expensive legal battle.
  Phòng ngừa: Sign SHA before accepting first cent of revenue.

#4 — Nhận tiền từ sai investor:
  Terms that are toxic; control provisions that hamstring future decisions.
  "Smart money" that turns out to be neither.
  Phòng ngừa: Reference check investors; understand terms fully; choose carefully.
```

---

**Category 2 — Finance và Legal:**

```
#5 — Vay vốn mà không hiểu terms:
  Hidden covenants; cross-default; variable rate in rising rate environment.
  Phòng ngừa: Read every clause; negotiate; engage financial advisor.

#6 — Personal guarantee không có limit:
  Personally liable for all company debt → Family assets at risk.
  Phòng ngừa: Negotiate cap on personal liability; or avoid personal guarantee.

#7 — Không có IP assignment cho tất cả contributors:
  Contractor or former employee owns IP your business depends on.
  Phòng ngừa: IP assignment agreement signed BEFORE any work begins.

#8 — Bán equity với liquidation preference unfavorable:
  Multiple liquidation preference; participating preferred → Company sold for 50 tỷ;
  investors get 45 tỷ; founders get 5 tỷ. Not what you imagined.
  Phòng ngừa: Understand liquidation stack before signing every round.

#9 — Tax fraud hoặc tax evasion:
  Criminal liability; company closure; personal prosecution.
  Phòng ngừa: Never. Optimize within law; không outside it.

#10 — Miss regulatory filing deadline:
  Some deadlines: Miss once → Cannot retroactively correct; company validity affected.
  Phòng ngừa: Calendar all regulatory deadlines; accounting software alerts.

#11 — Không đăng ký IP trước khi public:
  Patent; trademark; copyright → Cannot register after public disclosure in many cases.
  Phòng ngừa: File before launch; before press coverage; before public announcement.

#12 — Sign contract mà không đọc kỹ:
  Auto-renewal; unlimited liability; IP transfer; exclusivity you didn't intend.
  Phòng ngừa: Never sign anything without reading and understanding every clause.
```

---

**Category 3 — Team và People:**

```
#13 — Hire C-level executive sai vào early stage:
  Wrong VP Sales; VP Engineering at 10-person company → Takes 6-12 months to unwind.
  Cultural damage; team built around wrong person.
  Phòng ngừa: Take 3× longer to hire for senior roles. Multi-step interview process.

#14 — Fire wrongly (without valid legal basis):
  Labor lawsuit; reputation damage; team trust destroyed.
  Phòng ngừa: Document performance issues; follow HR process; consult lawyer.

#15 — Give large equity grants without vesting:
  Person leaves after 6 months with 10% equity; company forever has cap table problem.
  Phòng ngừa: All grants have 1-year cliff; 4-year vesting; reverse vesting for founders.

#16 — Publicly shame hoặc humiliate employee:
  VN startup community small; word spreads. Talented people won't join.
  Phòng ngừa: Feedback in private; recognition in public. Always.

#17 — Burn bridges with key talent when they leave:
  Former employees become customers; investors; referrers; potential acquirers.
  Phòng ngừa: Treat departures with dignity and generosity.

#18 — Promote tất cả technical talent to management:
  Best engineer → Bad manager → Both roles suffer.
  Phòng ngừa: Create IC (Individual Contributor) career track as viable alternative.
```

---

**Category 4 — Strategy và Product:**

```
#19 — Pivot toàn bộ vì 1 investor's suggestion:
  Abandon validated direction; build toward what someone external thinks is better.
  Phòng ngừa: Investor's opinions are input; not direction. You decide based on customer evidence.

#20 — Build core product trên third-party platform:
  Platform changes rules; shuts down; charges prohibitive fees → Your business at their mercy.
  Phòng ngừa: Diversify platform dependency; control your core technology.

#21 — Launch với broken product:
  First impression; word-of-mouth; App Store rating → Very hard to recover.
  Phòng ngừa: Soft launch; controlled beta; minimum stability bar before public launch.

#22 — Give away exclusivity in a market or category:
  "Exclusive partner in Vietnam for X" → Cannot explore other options for years.
  Phòng ngừa: Never; or extremely limited with clear term and performance conditions.

#23 — Close down too early:
  Many successful companies were on brink of failure before turning corner.
  Too early = Missed compounding returns of all prior investment.
  Phòng ngừa: Exhaust all options; consult widely; before deciding to close.

#24 — Scale before product-market fit:
  Spend millions acquiring customers who don't stay.
  Phòng ngừa: Prove retention at small scale before spending on acquisition.
```

---

**Category 5 — Relationship và Reputation:**

```
#25 — Lie to investor:
  VN startup community small; reputation permanent. Criminal fraud in extreme cases.
  Phòng ngừa: Never. Admit problems early; present plan; maintain trust.

#26 — Public dispute với co-founder hoặc investor:
  Signals instability; deters future investors; harms team confidence.
  Phòng ngừa: Resolve disputes privately; legally if necessary; quietly.

#27 — Commit fraud hoặc misrepresent business to customer:
  Legal liability; reputation damage; customer lawsuits.
  Phòng ngừa: Never. Over-deliver; never over-promise.

#28 — Cultural fit ignored in acquisition:
  Acquire company; cultures clash → Integration fails; acquired team leaves.
  Phòng ngừa: Cultural due diligence; integration planning before close.

#29 — Không trả đủ cho nhân viên khi công ty khó:
  Delayed; reduced; or missed payroll → Trust destroyed; legal liability; word spreads.
  Phòng ngừa: Payroll is most sacred obligation; protect it above all other expenses.

#30 — Không honor commitments to early employees:
  Equity promises; bonus; promotion → Not delivered.
  Phòng ngừa: Put everything in writing; honor every commitment; or renegotiate early.
```

---

**Category 6 — Operations và Scale:**

```
#31 — Mở too many locations/markets simultaneously:
  Cannot support each properly → All perform mediocre; brand damaged.
  Phòng ngừa: Depth before breadth; prove model in each market before next.

#32 — Commission sai pricing structure to sales team:
  Incentivize wrong behavior; long-term cultural problem.
  Phòng ngừa: Think through second-order effects of every incentive design.

#33 — Create two-tier team (office vs. remote; different benefits):
  Resentment; attrition; discrimination complaints.
  Phòng ngừa: Equity in benefits and opportunity across all employees.

#34 — Accept funding with ratchet clauses:
  If performance targets missed → Dilution penalty; founder equity decimated.
  Phòng ngừa: Understand every anti-dilution; ratchet; or performance provision fully.

#35 — Không backup data và mất toàn bộ:
  No backup → Database crash → All customer data gone → Company ends.
  Phòng ngừa: 3-2-1 backup; tested monthly.

#36 — Sign lease trên mặt bằng khi business model chưa proven:
  Multi-year lease obligation; business model fails; stuck with rent cost.
  Phòng ngừa: Short-term lease first; prove model; then commit long-term.

#37 — Over-invest in physical infrastructure too early:
  Office renovation; custom manufacturing line; specialized equipment.
  Before revenue validates → Sunk cost; impairs flexibility.
  Phòng ngừa: Lease before buy; flexible before fixed; always.

#38 — Không document institutional knowledge:
  Key person leaves; takes all knowledge → Operations disrupted.
  Phòng ngừa: Documentation culture; SOPs; wikis; regular knowledge transfer.

#39 — Release production code without testing:
  Data corruption; security breach; customer data loss → Trust destroyed.
  Phòng ngừa: Testing pipeline; staged deployment; rollback capability.

#40 — Không có disaster recovery plan cho critical systems:
  Server goes down; no recovery plan → Days of downtime → Customer exodus.
  Phòng ngừa: Document and TEST recovery procedure quarterly.

#41 — Bỏ qua regulatory warning letter lần đầu:
  "Nhỏ thôi; chưa cần lo" → Second violation; much larger penalty; public enforcement.
  Phòng ngừa: Every regulatory communication: Respond; comply; document.

#42 — Take on customer that is 50%+ of revenue với no switching cost:
  They know your dependency; use it in every negotiation.
  Phòng ngừa: Cap any single customer at < 20% of revenue; or have locked-in contracts.

#43 — Build dependent on one distribution channel:
  Facebook algorithm changes; App Store policy shift; Shopee policy change → Revenue cliff.
  Phòng ngừa: Multi-channel from early stage; own direct relationships.

#44 — Không mua bảo hiểm business liability:
  One customer lawsuit; data breach; employee accident → Uninsured exposure.
  Phòng ngừa: Business liability insurance from Day 1; review coverage annually.

#45 — Raise at valuation bạn không thể justify next round:
  Down round = Dilution; signal; team morale; option underwater.
  Phòng ngừa: Raise at sustainable valuation với realistic growth path.

#46 — Không secure competitive salary data trước khi hire:
  Overpay: Compress future hires' expectations và budget.
  Underpay: Lose candidates; or hire wrong people.
  Phòng ngừa: Market data (LinkedIn Salary; surveys) before any hire.

#47 — Announce product launch before it's ready:
  Press coverage when product broken → Permanent SEO record of bad reviews.
  Phòng ngừa: Embargo until launch-ready; không announce until live and stable.

#48 — Mix company và personal finances:
  Tax complications; legal liability; investor concerns; divorce complications.
  Phòng ngừa: Separate accounts from Day 1; no exceptions.

#49 — Không bảo vệ trade secret và confidential information:
  Employee leaves; takes customer list; pricing; strategy to competitor.
  Phòng ngừa: NDA; employment agreements; access controls; compartmentalization.

#50 — Không bao giờ tự review lại quyết định lớn:
  First major decisions rarely optimal; learning from mistakes is compound interest.
  Not reviewing = Not learning = Same mistakes repeated.
  Phòng ngừa: Quarterly decision journal; annual review of major decisions and outcomes.
```

> **Phân tích sâu:** Điều đáng chú ý về 50 sai lầm này: Phần lớn đều có thể avoid với đơn giản là slowing down và asking: "What's the worst that can happen? Is this reversible? Do I understand all the terms?" Không phải genius-level insight — chỉ là deliberate pause before irreversible action. That pause, consistently applied, is worth more than any amount of intelligence or experience.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến #1:** Assume you're immune from list like this. "Those are for inexperienced founders. I know better." Every founder who has experienced one of these catastrophic failures thought the same thing. Humility is the meta-skill that prevents most items on this list.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến #2:** Learn from your own mistakes only. Most of these 50 mistakes: Someone else in VN ecosystem has already made them. Find mentors; peer groups; advisors who have been through what you're approaching. Their experience is cheaper than yours.

> **Cạm bẫy:** Over-apply caution and never decide. This list is not meant to paralyze — it's meant to identify where extra care is warranted. For every reversible, small-stakes decision: Move fast. For irreversible; high-stakes decisions: Slow down; get input; understand terms; sleep on it. Calibrate speed to stakes.
