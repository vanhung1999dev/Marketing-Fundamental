### Chi Phí Nhân Công Liên Quan (Relevant Labour Costs)

Nhân công là chi phí phức tạp nhất để analyze — vì labour có đặc điểm "stickiness" (khó tăng giảm nhanh), nhiều component khác nhau, và often involves irreversible decisions.

> **Bài học:** Không phải mọi lao động hiện có đều là variable cost. Lao động "dôi dư" (idle time) là fixed đối với quyết định ngắn hạn. Lao động "thuê thêm" cho một order cụ thể là variable. Phân biệt này quyết định đúng/sai trong relevant cost analysis.

---

**Phân loại labour trong relevant cost context:**

**1. Idle labour (Lao động nhàn rỗi):**
- Nhân viên đang trả lương nhưng không có đủ việc làm
- Relevant cost cho thêm việc = ZERO (đang trả rồi, không tăng thêm)
- Exception: nếu còn làm được việc khác có giá trị → opportunity cost

```
Tình huống: 5 workers idle (capacity not fully used)
Decision: Accept special order cần 5 workers × 40h
  Direct labour cost = 5 × 40h × 50,000 VNĐ/h = 10 triệu
  But: workers already being paid → Relevant cost = 0
  (no incremental cost — they're being paid regardless)
```

**2. Labour without alternative use:**
- Hiện tại đang làm nhưng nếu bỏ việc cụ thể này → không có alternative work
- Relevant cost = basic pay (must still be paid)

**3. Labour requiring overtime:**
- Phải làm thêm giờ để complete order
- Relevant cost = Overtime premium (base pay already being paid for regular hours)
- Regular hours: relevant là 0 (already paid); OT premium: relevant là 100%

**4. Labour requiring new hire:**
- Cần tuyển thêm người cho order/project
- Relevant cost = full cost of new hire (recruitment + salary + benefits + training)

---

**Overtime Decision Framework:**

```
Tình huống: Đơn hàng cần thêm 100h lao động
Option A: Current workers overtime (150% pay)
Option B: Temp workers (contractor rate)
Option C: Delay other work (opportunity cost of CM lost)

Regular rate: 60,000 VNĐ/h
OT rate: 90,000 VNĐ/h
Contractor rate: 80,000 VNĐ/h
CM lost per hour from delayed work: 45,000 VNĐ/h

Comparison:
  Option A: 100h × 90,000 (OT premium only over base) = 9,000,000
  Actually: OT ADDITIONAL cost over base = (90K − 60K) × 100h = 3,000,000
  (if employees already scheduled → base is sunk; only premium is incremental)

  Option B: 100h × 80,000 = 8,000,000 (full cost — new resource)

  Option C: Opportunity cost = 100h × 45,000 = 4,500,000 (CM lost)

→ Option A cheapest if workers would otherwise be idle
→ Option C "cost" = 4.5M → accept this only if CM gained > 4.5M
```

**Thuê ngoài (Outsourcing) nhân công:**

Relevant cost analysis cho outsource vs insource:

| Cost element | In-house | Outsource |
|---|---|---|
| Lương và BHXH | Yes (variable if new hire) | Bundled in rate |
| Overhead (workspace, equipment) | Yes if dedicated | Usually included |
| Management time to supervise | Yes (hidden cost) | Less |
| Training investment | Yes — can be amortized | Vendor's cost |
| Quality control | In-house | Must monitor vendor |
| Flexibility | Lower (employment law) | Higher (contract end) |

**VN context:** Luật Lao động VN 2019 — nhân viên được bảo vệ chặt hơn → cost to terminate higher → makes labour more "fixed" than in flexible labour markets. This increases the value of: (a) hiring slowly but carefully, (b) using contractors for variable/seasonal demand.

---

**BHXH, BHYT, BHTN — true labour cost:**

Nhiều manager tính "labour cost = lương." Thực tế tại VN:

```
Gross salary:                    100%
  + BHXH employer (17.5%):        17.5%
  + BHYT employer (3%):            3%
  + BHTN employer (1%):            1%
  + Trade union (2%):              2%
  ─────────────────────────────────
Total employer cost:             123.5% of gross salary

Ví dụ: Lương gross 20 triệu/tháng
  True cost to company: 20 × 1.235 = 24.7 triệu/tháng
```

**Plus:** Tuyển dụng cost, onboarding, space, equipment, benefits → true FTE cost thường 1.3–1.5× gross salary.

> **Phân tích sâu:** Studies on organisational restructuring show: companies that reduce headcount as primary cost response typically re-hire within 18–24 months as business recovers — often at higher cost (recruitment, training, temporary premium). Better cost discipline during growth (not over-hiring) prevents the painful cycle of hire-fire-hire. VN context: phải trả trợ cấp thôi việc (6 months per year of service for older law, 1/2 month per year for Bộ luật Lao động 2019), BHXH implications → firing expensive → discipline in hiring decisions critical.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến #1:** Accept a project without costing the management time required to oversee it. Management time is a scarce, expensive resource (implicit cost). A project requiring 2 weeks of CFO time at "cost" of zero ignores that those 2 weeks could have been used for higher-value activities. Always explicitly cost management bandwidth.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến #2:** Ignore training investment in insource vs outsource. "In-house cheaper — just need to pay salary." But new employee needs 3–6 months to be productive. Training cost + productivity ramp are real costs. Outsource vendor is immediately productive (you're paying their training cost via higher rate). For short-duration, specialist work: outsource often cheaper TCO.

> **Cạm bẫy:** Treat BHXH as "not my problem" when hiring contractors. Nếu nhà thầu thực chất là nhân viên (theo tiêu chí pháp lý: working hours, supervision, exclusivity), cơ quan BHXH có thể yêu cầu backpay contributions. Risk: audit finds contractor relationship is actually employment → significant BHXH liability + penalties.
