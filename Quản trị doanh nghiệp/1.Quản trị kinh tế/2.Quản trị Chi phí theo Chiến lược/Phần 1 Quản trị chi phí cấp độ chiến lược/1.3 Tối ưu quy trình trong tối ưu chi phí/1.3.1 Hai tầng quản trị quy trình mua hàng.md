### Hai Tầng Quản Trị Quy Trình Mua Hàng

Mua hàng (procurement) là một trong những lever quan trọng nhất trong cost management — đặc biệt với manufacturing và trading companies, nơi COGS chiếm 50–70% doanh thu. Quản trị procurement tốt có thể improve margin 3–8 percentage points.

> **Bài học:** Procurement không phải là "đặt hàng và trả tiền" — là strategic function liên quan đến supplier relationships, negotiation, risk management, và quality control. Phân tách hai tầng strategic vs operational cho phép từng tầng được manage đúng mức.

---

**Hai tầng Procurement:**

**Tầng 1 — Strategic Procurement:**
- Supplier selection và qualification
- Contract negotiation và management
- Category management (grouping similar purchases)
- Supplier development và relationship management
- Make vs Buy analysis
- Supply chain risk assessment

*Ai làm:* Senior procurement, Category managers
*Frequency:* Annually hoặc per contract cycle

**Tầng 2 — Operational Procurement (P2P — Procure to Pay):**
- Requisition creation
- Purchase Order (PO) issuance
- Goods receipt và inspection
- Invoice matching (3-way match: PO ↔ GR ↔ Invoice)
- Payment processing
- Supplier query resolution

*Ai làm:* Procurement clerks, Accounts Payable team
*Frequency:* Daily

---

**Tại sao phải tách hai tầng:**

| Không tách | Tách rõ ràng |
|---|---|
| Senior manager bị distract bởi operational POs | Senior focus on strategic negotiation, relationships |
| Quyết định supplier bị ảnh hưởng bởi ai đang urgently need PO today | Supplier selection objective, based on long-term criteria |
| Không ai có accountability cho category spend tổng thể | Category manager owns total spend, tracks performance |
| Controls yếu — conflict of interest possible | Segregation of duties: requestor ≠ approver ≠ payer |

---

**P2P Process và 3-Way Match:**

```
Bước 1: Requisition (yêu cầu mua hàng) → approved by manager
Bước 2: PO issued to supplier → signed contract
Bước 3: Goods received → GR recorded by warehouse
Bước 4: Invoice received from supplier
Bước 5: 3-way match: PO quantity/price = GR quantity = Invoice amount
Bước 6: If matched → approve for payment
         If not matched → hold → investigate → resolve
```

**3-Way Match prevents:**
- Paying for goods not received
- Paying at wrong price
- Duplicate invoice payment

---

**Category Management — organize spend:**

Thay vì manage individual suppliers separately, group purchases into categories và assign category manager:

| Category | Spend ví dụ | Strategy |
|---|---|---|
| Raw materials | NVL chính — chiếm >50% COGS | Long-term contracts, strategic relationships, hedging |
| Packaging | Hộp, túi, nhãn mác | Volume leverage, standardize specs |
| MRO (maintenance, repair, operations) | Dụng cụ, phụ tùng | Consolidate suppliers, preferred vendor list |
| Professional services | Luật sư, tư vấn, kiểm toán | Annual review, competitive tender |
| IT & Technology | Software, hardware, cloud | Subscription vs capex; total cost of ownership |

---

**Supplier Performance Management:**

```
SCORECARD — quarterly review với key suppliers:
□ On-time delivery rate (target: >95%)
□ Quality acceptance rate (target: >99%)
□ Price competitiveness vs market
□ Responsiveness to issues
□ ESG compliance (environmental, labour practices)

Tier the suppliers:
  Strategic (1–3): Partner deeply; share roadmaps; joint development
  Preferred (4–10): Preferred vendor list; competitive but stable
  Transactional (>10): Competitive bidding; price-focused
```

> **Phân tích sâu:** IKEA's procurement model là benchmark: Category managers globally negotiate framework contracts với key suppliers; local operations call off from these contracts. Result: scale leverage globally, flexibility locally. VN companies với multiple locations often miss this — each site negotiates separately → lose scale advantage. Centralizing category strategy while decentralizing operational execution is the sweet spot.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến #1:** Chỉ focus vào price mà bỏ qua Total Cost of Ownership (TCO). Supplier A: 100,000/unit. Supplier B: 95,000/unit. Nhưng supplier B có: 5% defect rate → rework cost; 20% late delivery rate → production stoppage cost; requires quality inspection → inspection cost. TCO of supplier B cao hơn. Price ≠ cost.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến #2:** Không có written contracts với key suppliers. "Mình quen biết, không cần hợp đồng." Khi giá nguyên liệu tăng, supplier tăng giá không báo trước. Khi chất lượng kém, không có SLA để hold accountable. Contract là protection cho cả hai bên.

> **Cạm bẫy:** Single-source dependency — chỉ có một supplier cho critical material. Convenience ngắn hạn nhưng risk cao: supplier có pricing power, supply disruption = production halt. Nguyên tắc: key materials should have at least 2 qualified suppliers; primary gets 70–80% share; backup gets 20–30% và được tested regularly.
