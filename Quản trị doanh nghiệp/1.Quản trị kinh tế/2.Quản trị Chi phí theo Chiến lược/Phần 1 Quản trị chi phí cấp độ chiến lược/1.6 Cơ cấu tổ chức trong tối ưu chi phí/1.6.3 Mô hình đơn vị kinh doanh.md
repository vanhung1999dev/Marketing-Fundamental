### Mô Hình Đơn Vị Kinh Doanh (Business Unit Model)

Business Unit (BU) là một bộ phận của tổ chức hoạt động như một "mini-company" với full P&L accountability — own revenue, own costs, own performance measurement. Mô hình BU tạo ra entrepreneurial culture và cost accountability trong tổ chức lớn.

> **Bài học:** BU model không phải "chia nhỏ công ty" — là tạo ra clear ownership cho từng business, với autonomy đủ để operate efficiently và accountability rõ ràng về kết quả. Key tension: autonomy (freedom to operate) vs alignment (consistency with group strategy).

---

**BU as Mini P&L:**

Mỗi BU có P&L riêng:

```
BU Revenue                                   100 tỷ
  − Direct COGS (gắn trực tiếp với BU)       (65 tỷ)
  = Gross Profit                               35 tỷ    (35%)
  − Direct OpEx (BU-owned: Sales, BU Mgmt)   (15 tỷ)
  = BU Contribution Margin                    20 tỷ    (20%)
  − Allocated overhead từ Group               (8 tỷ)
  = BU Operating Profit (EBIT)                12 tỷ    (12%)
```

**Two-level P&L:** Contribution margin (trước group overhead) là BU's "controllable" measure. EBIT after allocation là "full" measure.

---

**Autonomy vs Alignment — the eternal tension:**

| Autonomy cao | Alignment cao |
|---|---|
| BU decides own strategy | Group strategy cascades down |
| BU hires own people | Group-wide hiring standards |
| BU buys own systems | Standardized systems across group |
| BU sets own prices | Pricing framework from center |
| Fast decisions locally | Consistent brand/quality |

**Finding the balance:**

```
Centralize:
  □ Group brand and identity
  □ Key risk policies (financial, legal, compliance)
  □ Capital allocation (large investments)
  □ Shared services (finance, IT, HR)

Decentralize:
  □ Customer relationships and sales
  □ Product/service customization
  □ Day-to-day operations
  □ Hiring (within standards)
```

---

**Transfer Pricing giữa BUs:**

Khi BU A cung cấp service cho BU B, cần cơ chế pricing để:
- Không distort BU A's cost (under-charging)
- Không distort BU B's cost (over-charging)
- Create right incentives for both

**Common approaches:**

| Method | Scenario | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Market price | External market exists | IT support: charged at market rate của outsource option |
| Cost + 5% | No market price; internal only | Legal services: cost + small markup |
| Cost only | Pure support without profit intent | Finance shared services: fully at cost |
| Negotiated | Complex dependencies | Manufacturing BU charges Distribution BU for product |

---

**Khi nào nên decentralize sang BU model:**

| Signal | Indicator |
|---|---|
| **Diversity** | 2+ very different business lines (khác customer, product, channel) |
| **Scale** | Revenue >100 tỷ với multiple clear segments |
| **Geography** | Multiple regions với different market dynamics |
| **Accountability need** | Cần clearer ownership for P&L performance |
| **Speed need** | Centralized decisions too slow for market responsiveness |

**Khi không nên decentralize:**
- Business too small — overhead of BU structure > benefits
- Business tightly integrated — impossible to separate P&L cleanly
- Key skills concentrated in few people — can't staff multiple BU leadership teams

---

**Performance Management trong BU model:**

```
BU Head objectives:
  1. Revenue target (growth)
  2. Contribution margin target (efficiency)
  3. ROIC (capital efficiency) — nếu BU has own assets
  4. Strategic KPIs (customer satisfaction, market share)

Group evaluation:
  Monthly: Revenue and contribution vs target
  Quarterly: BU review with Group CEO
  Annually: Strategic plan update + full P&L review
```

> **Phân tích sâu:** GE under Jack Welch's "Work-Out" and BU accountability model became benchmark for corporate governance. But GE Capital (huge financial BU) accumulated risks that headquarters didn't understand or control → 2008 crisis exposure. Lesson: BU autonomy requires adequate Group oversight of risk concentration — not micro-management, but clear risk boundaries và capital allocation governance.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến #1:** Create BU structure without clean P&L. If shared costs can't be allocated fairly → BU heads fight about allocations → no one takes ownership of full P&L → BU model fails. Must have: clean revenue attribution, direct cost tracking, and agreed overhead allocation methodology BEFORE launching BU structure.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến #2:** BU head given P&L accountability but not given matching authority. "You're responsible for profit but I decide headcount, pricing, and capital." Responsibility without authority → frustration → disengagement. Autonomy và accountability must be matched.

> **Cạm bẫy:** BUs competing against each other for Group resources và opportunities. When Group Capital allocation is zero-sum game → BU heads become political rather than entrepreneurial → gaming the planning process → misleading data. Group must have transparent, objective capital allocation criteria known to all BU heads.
