### Quan Điểm Định Giá Của Nhà Đầu Tư

Các trường phái đầu tư khác nhau nhìn định giá theo cách khác nhau — không có "đúng duy nhất," mỗi approach phù hợp với investment philosophy khác nhau.

> **Bài học:** Biết investor của mình là ai và họ define "value" như thế nào. Angel investor đánh giá differently với PE fund, VC, hoặc thị trường chứng khoán. Chuẩn bị câu chuyện khác nhau cho từng loại.

---

**Các trường phái đầu tư:**

**1. Value Investing (Buffett/Graham):**
- Tìm businesses với intrinsic value > market price (margin of safety)
- Focus: P/B thấp, P/E thấp, strong balance sheet, consistent ROE
- Câu hỏi: "Business này worth gì nếu tôi buy toàn bộ hôm nay?"
- VN examples: Ngân hàng, steel, F&B mature companies — thường trade at value multiples

**2. Growth Investing:**
- Sẵn sàng pay premium cho companies với high growth potential
- Focus: Revenue growth rate, market size (TAM), unit economics, NRR (Net Revenue Retention)
- Câu hỏi: "Nếu company hit target trong 3–5 năm, valuation nào justified?"
- VN examples: Tech startups, fintech, edtech

**3. GARP — Growth at Reasonable Price:**
- Tìm growth companies nhưng không pay unlimited premium
- PEG Ratio < 1 là sweet spot
- Warren Buffett later evolution

**4. Income Investing:**
- Tìm consistent, high dividend yield
- Focus: dividend sustainability, payout ratio, FCF coverage
- VN: utilities, mature consumer companies, ngân hàng lớn

**5. Momentum Investing:**
- Mua những gì đang tăng, bán những gì đang giảm
- Ít liên quan đến fundamentals, nhiều đến price action
- Not fundamental-based

---

**Góc nhìn PE (Private Equity) — đặc thù:**

PE investors nhìn private company valuations với lens khác:

```
PE Entry Thesis:
  1. What multiple am I paying? (EV/EBITDA, Revenue)
  2. Can I improve margins? (operations improvement)
  3. Can I grow revenue? (new markets, products, M&A)
  4. What exit multiple can I expect? (re-rating)
  5. Leverage: can I enhance returns with debt?

Returns Model:
  IRR = f(Entry multiple, EBITDA growth, Margin expansion, Exit multiple, Leverage)
```

PE target: 20–25% IRR (Internal Rate of Return) over 3–7 year hold period.

---

**Góc nhìn VC (Venture Capital):**

VC model acknowledge most investments fail → need outsized returns on winners:

```
Power Law in VC:
  - 50% investments: write-off (0x return)
  - 40% investments: 1–5x return
  - 9% investments: 5–20x return
  - 1% investments: 50–100x+ return → carry the entire fund

VC valuation: less about current DCF, more about:
  - Market size (TAM must be $1B+ for VC to be interested)
  - Team quality
  - Traction metrics (user growth, retention, engagement)
  - Path to dominance
```

VN VC market đang active: Sequoia India, Jungle Ventures, Do Ventures, Monk's Hill, VSV Capital — focus on edtech, fintech, e-commerce, healthtech.

**Khi nào BCTC quan trọng nhất với từng loại investor:**

| Investor | BCTC importance | Họ muốn thấy |
|---|---|---|
| **Bank** | Rất cao | DSCR, collateral, historical earnings, leverage |
| **PE/Strategic** | Rất cao | EBITDA, FCF, ROIC, clean books |
| **Angel/Early VC** | Thấp | Team, traction, market size |
| **Late-stage VC** | Trung bình | Unit economics, path to profitability |
| **Public market** | Cao | EPS growth, revenue growth, margins |

**Kỳ vọng return theo loại đầu tư VN:**

| Loại đầu tư | Expected Return | Risk Level |
|---|---|---|
| Government bond | 5–6% | Very low |
| Blue chip stocks (VNM, MBB) | 12–15% including dividend | Low–Medium |
| Mid-cap stocks | 15–25% | Medium–High |
| PE/Private equity | 20–30% | High |
| VC (expected across portfolio) | 25%+ (pool) | Very high |
| Angel (individual deal) | 30–50%+ | Extreme |

*Note:* Expected return higher → actual distribution wider → more uncertainty.

> **Phân tích sâu:** Nghiên cứu của Aswath Damodaran (NYU, "guru of valuation") cho thấy: valuation is not about finding the "right" number, it's about identifying the key value drivers and stress-testing them. For most businesses, 3 variables drive 80% of value: (1) revenue growth rate, (2) target operating margin, (3) cost of capital. Get these right, and precision in other inputs doesn't matter much. Focus your effort where it matters.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến #1:** Present single-point valuation to investors. Sophisticated investors immediately ask: "What are your assumptions? What's the sensitivity?" Present a range with scenarios (bear/base/bull) and show how value changes. Shows intellectual honesty and analytical rigor.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến #2:** Base valuation entirely on multiples without fundamentals. "Similar company was valued at 5x revenue, so we're worth 5x revenue." Multiples are shortcuts — they imply DCF assumptions. If your business doesn't justify those DCF assumptions (different growth, different margin, different risk), the multiple doesn't apply.

> **Cạm bẫy:** Anchoring bias — founder anchors on first valuation discussed and resists adjusting even when fundamentals change. Market conditions, interest rates, and comparable transactions change. Valuation is not a fixed number — it moves with facts and market conditions. Being flexible and analytical about valuation (rather than emotionally attached) is a professional advantage in fundraising.
