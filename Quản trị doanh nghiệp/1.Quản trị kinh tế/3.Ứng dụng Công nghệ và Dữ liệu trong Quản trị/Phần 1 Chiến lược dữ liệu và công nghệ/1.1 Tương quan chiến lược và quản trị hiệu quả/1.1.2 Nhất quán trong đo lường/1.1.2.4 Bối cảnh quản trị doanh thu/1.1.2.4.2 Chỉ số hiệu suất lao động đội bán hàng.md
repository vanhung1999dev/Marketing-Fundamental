### Chỉ Số Hiệu Suất Lao Động Đội Bán Hàng

Đo lường hiệu suất salespeople là nghệ thuật cân bằng: đủ để track accountability, không quá nhiều đến mức micromanage. Phải include cả leading indicators (activity) và lagging indicators (revenue) để có full picture về sức khỏe của team.

> **Bài học:** "Revenue attainment" một mình không đủ để manage salespeople. Một rep có thể hit Q1 revenue bằng cách pull forward deals từ Q2 → Q2 miss. Hoặc hit revenue bằng cách sell kém profitable deals. Composite scorecard beats any single metric.

---

**Quota Attainment — nền tảng:**

```
Quota Attainment = Actual Revenue / Quota × 100%

Distribution trong healthy team:
  >120%: Stars (10–15% of team)
  100–120%: Strong performers (25–35%)
  80–100%: On track (30–40%)
  <80%: At risk / need coaching (20–30%)
  <60% for 2 consecutive quarters: Performance managed

Red flags:
  >80% team consistently hitting 90–100%: quota quá dễ (sandbagging)
  <50% team hitting quota: quota unrealistic, skill issue, or market issue
```

---

**Activity Metrics — Leading Indicators quan trọng:**

| Metric | Why Matters | Weekly Target (B2B) |
|---|---|---|
| # Outbound calls/emails | Top-of-funnel effort | 50–80/week |
| # Qualified meetings booked | Pipeline building | 8–12/week |
| # Demos/presentations | Conversion effort | 4–6/week |
| # Proposals submitted | Late-stage effort | 2–4/week |
| # Follow-ups on open opps | Velocity management | All opps touched weekly |

Note: Targets depend heavily on deal size/complexity. Enterprise: fewer but deeper engagements. SMB: higher volume.

---

**Time Allocation Analysis:**

Research cho thấy salespeople chỉ dành 30–40% thời gian thực sự selling:

```
Typical distribution:
  Selling activities (calls, meetings, demos): 35%
  Admin (CRM updates, reporting, emails): 25%
  Internal meetings: 20%
  Travel/transit: 12%
  Training: 8%

Goal: Increase selling time to 50–60%
Solutions:
  - Automate CRM data entry (AI tools, auto-logging)
  - Reduce internal meetings (async updates)
  - Sales ops team handles admin burdens
  - Efficient routing for field sales reps
```

---

**Ramp Period Management:**

```
Typical new rep ramp curve:
  Month 1–2: Training; 0% quota expected
  Month 3: 25–40% quota attainment
  Month 4–5: 50–70% quota attainment
  Month 6+: 80–100% full quota

Ramp time longer when:
  - Product highly complex
  - Long sales cycles
  - Cold territory (no existing relationships)
  - Insufficient manager support and coaching
```

---

**Composite Sales Scorecard:**

| Metric | Weight | Healthy | At risk |
|---|---|---|---|
| Quota attainment | 40% | >90% | <70% |
| Pipeline coverage | 20% | >3x | <2x |
| Activity level | 15% | On target | <70% of target |
| Win rate | 15% | At/above team avg | >10% below avg |
| Average deal size | 10% | Stable or growing | Declining trend |

> **Phân tích sâu:** Salesforce research: high-performing sales organizations are 2.8× more likely to use data and analytics to inform decisions than underperforming ones. Nhưng equally important: top performers cũng spend more time coaching. Best sales managers spend 50%+ time coaching vs managing admin/reporting. Metric collection phải enable coaching conversations — not replace human judgment with dashboards.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến #1:** Chỉ track revenue, không track activity metrics cho new reps. New rep có thể có 0 revenue trong tháng 1–3 nhưng excellent activity metrics → on track. Hoặc 0 revenue VÀ low activity → intervention ngay. Without activity metrics, bạn discover problem quá muộn (at Q1 close, not week 3).

> **Sai lầm phổ biến #2:** Same quota cho tất cả reps bất kể territory, seniority, hay product. Rep thừa hưởng mature territory với existing customers có quota appropriateness rất khác rep đang build từ đầu. Individualized quota-setting creates fair accountability và realistic expectations.

> **Cạm bẫy:** Track activity quantity mà không quality. 100 cold calls/week nhưng không có meetings → poor targeting hoặc messaging vấn đề. Quality metrics cần thêm: meeting acceptance rate, demo-to-proposal conversion, proposal quality score (reviewed by manager). Quantity without quality = busy work, not productive work.
