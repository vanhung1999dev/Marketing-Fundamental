### Chỉ Số Tổng Quan Quy Trình Bán Hàng

Sales funnel metrics cho phép đo lường từng giai đoạn của quy trình bán hàng — từ lead đầu tiên đến deal thành công — giúp xác định chính xác bottleneck và ưu tiên cải tiến.

> **Bài học:** Doanh thu cuối kỳ là kết quả của hàng trăm micro-decisions trong funnel. Nếu chỉ track doanh thu, không biết vấn đề ở đâu. Nếu track funnel metrics, biết chính xác: ít lead? Lead kém chất lượng? Win rate thấp? Deal size nhỏ? Mỗi câu trả lời có giải pháp khác nhau.

---

**Sales Funnel tiêu chuẩn B2B:**

```
MQL (Marketing Qualified Lead)
  [Handoff: Marketing → Sales]
SQL (Sales Qualified Lead)
  [Qualification: BANT — Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline]
OPPORTUNITY / PROPOSAL
  [Proposal sent, negotiation begins]
VERBAL / COMMITTED
  [Verbal yes, contract being drafted]
CLOSED WON
```

---

**Pipeline Velocity — metric tổng hợp quan trọng nhất:**

```
Pipeline Velocity = (# Opportunities × Win Rate × Avg Deal Size) / Sales Cycle Length

Ví dụ:
  # Active opportunities: 80
  Win rate: 25%
  Average deal size: 200 triệu
  Sales cycle: 60 ngày

Velocity = (80 × 25% × 200M) / 60 = 66.7 triệu/ngày
Monthly revenue potential = 66.7 × 30 = ~2 tỷ/tháng

Cải thiện BẤT KỲ component nào 10% → velocity tăng 10%
```

---

**Pipeline Coverage Ratio:**

```
Pipeline Coverage = Total Pipeline Value / Revenue Target

Ví dụ: Target 10 tỷ/tháng, Win Rate 25%
  Pipeline needed = 10 tỷ / 25% = 40 tỷ
  Coverage ratio = 40 tỷ / 10 tỷ = 4x

Rule of thumb: Coverage ratio phải là 3–4× target
  Win rate 25% → cần 4x pipeline
  Win rate 33% → cần 3x pipeline
```

**Stage-by-stage benchmark B2B:**

| Funnel Stage | Key Metric | Benchmark |
|---|---|---|
| MQL generated | # MQLs/month | Depends on market size |
| MQL→SQL | SQL conversion rate | 20–40% |
| SQL→Opportunity | Qualification rate | 50–70% |
| Opportunity→Proposal | Proposal rate | 60–80% |
| Proposal→Close | Win rate | 20–35% |
| Overall | End-to-end conversion (MQL to Close) | 4–14% |

---

**Forecasting từ pipeline (weighted pipeline method):**

```
Expected Revenue = Sum(Deal Value × Stage Probability)

Stage probabilities (adjust for your actual win rates):
  SQL: 10%
  Proposal: 30%
  Committed: 70%
  Verbal: 90%
  Closed Won: 100%

Ví dụ:
  5 deals × 500M @ SQL      = 250M expected
  3 deals × 800M @ Proposal = 720M expected
  2 deals × 300M @ Committed = 420M expected
  Total expected this month: 1.39 tỷ
```

> **Phân tích sâu:** Gartner research: B2B buyers complete 57–70% of buying process before engaging sales. By the time an SQL exists in CRM, the prospect has formed significant opinions. Implication: top-of-funnel content (thought leadership, case studies, comparison guides) affects win rates even before sales team involvement. Marketing investment in content pays off in higher SQL quality and win rate — not just lead volume.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến #1:** Sales team inflates pipeline với wishful deals. "Tôi đã nói chuyện với họ 2 lần → họ trong pipeline." Disciplined qualification (BANT: Budget xác nhận, Authority — đúng người quyết định, Need — real pain, Timeline — trong 6 tháng) prevents pipeline from being filled with non-deals. Smaller, higher-quality pipeline beats large inflated one for forecasting.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến #2:** Review pipeline chỉ monthly. Pipeline là living asset — deals move, stall, fall out, hoặc accelerate hàng ngày. Weekly pipeline review với CRM data enables timely interventions. Monthly review means problems discovered too late to act.

> **Cạm bẫy:** Win rate calculated incorrectly — exclude "no decision" losses. True win rate = Won / (Won + Lost to competitor + No decision + Disqualified). "No decision" (customer decided to not buy at all) is often the majority of losses in B2B và phải được counted. Ignoring it makes win rate look artificially high.
