### Hệ Thống Dữ Liệu Trong Quản Trị

Dữ liệu không tự nhiên có giá trị — giá trị đến từ khả năng truy cập, tích hợp, và phân tích dữ liệu đúng lúc, đúng người, đúng định dạng. Hiểu kiến trúc dữ liệu giúp doanh nghiệp đầu tư đúng chỗ thay vì mua công nghệ không phù hợp với giai đoạn.

> **Bài học:** SME không cần Data Lake của Google. SME cần: (1) Hệ thống giao dịch chính xác (ERP/CRM/POS), (2) Một nơi để aggregate và report, (3) Người đọc và act on data. Complexity should match company maturity, not aspiration.

---

**Data Sources — nguồn dữ liệu:**

**Transactional (Internal):**
- ERP: tài chính, inventory, PO, production orders
- CRM: leads, customers, deals, activities
- POS: bán hàng tại điểm bán lẻ
- HRMS: nhân sự, lương, chấm công
- WMS (Warehouse Management): kho bãi, logistics

**Operational/Sensor:**
- IoT sensors: nhiệt độ, máy móc, supply chain tracking
- Website/App analytics: Google Analytics 4, Firebase
- Social media: engagement, mentions, sentiment

**External:**
- Fiinpro/FiinGroup: VN stocks và corporate financials
- GSO (Tổng cục Thống kê): macro indicators
- Industry benchmarks: VCCI, sector associations
- Competitor pricing: market research, web scraping (within legal limits)

---

**Data Architecture Options:**

| Architecture | Mô tả | Tốt cho |
|---|---|---|
| **Data Warehouse** | Structured, clean, pre-processed; optimized for OLAP queries | Reporting, dashboards, standard analytics |
| **Data Lake** | Raw data bất kỳ format; process on demand | Advanced analytics, ML, data exploration |
| **Data Lakehouse** | Hybrid: flexibility of lake + structure of warehouse | Modern enterprise (Databricks, Delta Lake) |

Risk của Data Lake: trở thành "data swamp" — data dump mà nobody knows how to use.

---

**SME Data Architecture — practical roadmap:**

```
Stage 1 (Revenue <50 tỷ):
  Source: MISA + Excel + CRM (HubSpot free tier)
  Reporting: Monthly export → Google Sheets dashboard
  Investment: Minimal; biggest ROI = getting source systems right
  Data team: Part-time analyst (Finance or Ops background)

Stage 2 (Revenue 50–500 tỷ):
  Source: ERP (MISA SME / SAP B1) + CRM + HRMS
  Reporting: Power BI / Looker Studio connected to ERP
  Data team: 1 dedicated analyst

Stage 3 (Revenue >500 tỷ):
  Source: Multiple enterprise systems
  Platform: Cloud data warehouse (BigQuery / Snowflake)
  ETL: Automated pipelines
  Data team: 3–5 (engineer + analyst + scientist)
```

---

**Data Silos — common VN SME problem:**

```
Typical VN SME silo:
  Finance: MISA with their numbers
  Sales: Own Excel/Zalo with customer list
  Operations: Paper-based or WhatsApp for production tracking
  HR: Different spreadsheet
  CEO: Asks for reports → each team exports own version → different numbers

Solution sequence:
  1. Agree on source of truth for each data type
  2. ERP for finance/ops; CRM for customer/sales; HRMS for people
  3. Connect them (API, export/import, or middleware)
  4. Create unified reporting layer (one dashboard everyone uses)
  5. Decommission shadow spreadsheets
```

---

**Data Freshness — how often does data need updating?**

| Decision type | Freshness needed | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Operational | Real-time or hourly | Inventory reorder triggers, production alerts |
| Tactical | Daily | Revenue vs target, pipeline status |
| Strategic | Weekly/Monthly | P&L analysis, market trend |

Cost of real-time infrastructure is much higher than batch. Only build real-time pipelines where real-time decisions are actually being made. Don't stream data reviewed monthly.

> **Phân tích sâu:** McKinsey research: data-driven organizations are 23× more likely to acquire customers, 6× as likely to retain customers, và 19× as likely to be profitable. But "data-driven" không phải là having more data — là having the right data, available to the right people, in time to make better decisions. Company với 100 well-curated data points used in decisions beats company với 10,000 in a data lake nobody accesses.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến #1:** Mua expensive BI tools trước khi fix data quality trong source systems. Power BI connected to bad data = beautiful wrong dashboards. Sequence đúng: Fix source data quality → Connect systems → Build reports → Build advanced analytics. Không được skip bước.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến #2:** Build data infrastructure cho công ty bạn aspire to be, không phải công ty bạn đang là. 30-person company không cần Snowflake và 5-person data team. MISA + Google Sheets + một part-time analyst is often sufficient và dramatically more actionable.

> **Cạm bẫy:** Data lake becomes data swamp. Không có clear use cases và active data governance, data lakes accumulate raw data nobody knows how to use. Mỗi project adds data nhưng nobody cleans up. Result: petabytes of storage, zero insight. Build data lake chỉ khi có clear analytical use cases và talent để develop them.
