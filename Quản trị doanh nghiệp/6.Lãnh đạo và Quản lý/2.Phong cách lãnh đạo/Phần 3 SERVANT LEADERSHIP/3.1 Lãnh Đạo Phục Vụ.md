### Lãnh Đạo Phục Vụ

Robert Greenleaf (1970): "The servant-leader is servant first." Leader tồn tại để serve team — remove obstacles, provide resources, shield từ organizational noise, create conditions for team to thrive.

**Test of servant leadership:** "Do those served grow as persons? Do they, while being served, become healthier, wiser, freer, more autonomous, more likely to become servants themselves?"

**Practical servant leadership behaviors:**
- Ask: "Có gì đang block bạn? Tôi có thể giúp gì?"
- Shield team từ unnecessary meetings, politics, noise
- Give credit publicly, take blame privately
- Make decisions that benefit team long-term, even if uncomfortable short-term
- Be accessible và genuinely interested in team members as people

> **Bài học:** Servant leadership không phải weakness hay lack of direction. Leader với clear vision và strong direction, who serves their team in achieving that vision, is the most effective combination. "Serve" không có nghĩa là không có standards — có nghĩa là support people trong meeting those standards.

> **Phân tích sâu:** **Trust equation** (David Maister): Trust = (Credibility + Reliability + Intimacy) / Self-orientation. Servant leaders have high Credibility (họ know their stuff), Reliability (họ follow through), và Intimacy (họ genuinely care) — và critically low Self-orientation (họ không focused vào their own agenda). Low self-orientation is the biggest differentiator của truly trusted leaders.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến:** Confuse servant leadership với pushover leadership. Servant leader still sets high standards, gives hard feedback, makes difficult calls — nhưng they do it IN SERVICE OF team's growth, không phải for their own ego hay comfort.

> **Cạm bẫy:** Servant leadership được adopt as philosophy mà không có practical behaviors change. "Tôi là servant leader" + still micromanage + not accessible + take credit = không phải servant leadership. Đây là style được lived, không được proclaimed.
