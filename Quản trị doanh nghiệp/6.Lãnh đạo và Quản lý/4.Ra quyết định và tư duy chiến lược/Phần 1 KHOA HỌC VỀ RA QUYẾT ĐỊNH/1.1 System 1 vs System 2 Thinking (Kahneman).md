### System 1 vs System 2 Thinking (Kahneman)

Daniel Kahneman (Thinking, Fast and Slow) mô tả hai hệ thống thinking:

**System 1 — Fast, automatic:**
- Intuitive, emotional, pattern-matching
- Runs unconsciously, low effort
- Great for routine decisions với familiar patterns
- Prone to biases và shortcuts

**System 2 — Slow, deliberate:**
- Analytical, logical, effortful
- Requires conscious attention
- Better cho complex, high-stakes, novel decisions
- Can be overridden by System 1 when tired/stressed

> **Bài học:** Hầu hết business decisions xảy ra trong System 1 — người feel họ đang thinking carefully nhưng thực ra là pattern-matching to past experience. Cho high-stakes decisions, deliberately engage System 2: slow down, structure the decision, seek disconfirming evidence, sleep on it.

> **Phân tích sâu:** **Ego Depletion** — System 2 thinking consumes mental energy. Khi tired hoặc decision-fatigued, mọi người default to System 1 — ngay cả cho important decisions. This is why: important meetings không nên schedule sau nhiều hours of difficult work, major decisions không nên be made khi exhausted, Barack Obama wore same clothes every day để reduce decision fatigue cho bigger decisions.
