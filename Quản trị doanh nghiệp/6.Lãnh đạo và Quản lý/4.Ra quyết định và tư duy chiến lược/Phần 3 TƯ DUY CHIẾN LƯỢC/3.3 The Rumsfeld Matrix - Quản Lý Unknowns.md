### The Rumsfeld Matrix — Quản Lý Unknowns

Donald Rumsfeld's framework:
- **Known Knowns:** Things we know we know — plan for these
- **Known Unknowns:** Things we know we don't know — research và scenario plan
- **Unknown Unknowns:** Things we don't know we don't know — build resilience và optionality

> **Bài học:** Strategy work thường focus entirely trên Known Knowns (our plan) và Known Unknowns (our risks). Unknown Unknowns — black swans, disruptive surprises — are where big failures occur. You can't predict them, nhưng có thể build organization resilient enough to survive và adapt. Redundancy, optionality, financial buffer = unknown unknown insurance.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến:** Strategy plans với single-point forecasts — "chúng ta sẽ grow 30% năm tới." Reality is range of scenarios. Better: scenario planning với 3 cases (base, upside, downside) và clear triggers cho switching between strategies.

> **Cạm bẫy:** Analysis paralysis — over-investing in information gathering trước khi decide. Jeff Bezos: "Most decisions should be made with somewhere around 70% of the information you wish you had. If you wait for 90%, in most cases, you're probably being slow." Know when enough information is enough.
