### Pyramid Principle (Barbara Minto — McKinsey)

**Bottom-up thinking, top-down communication.**

Khi think through problem: start with details, work to conclusion.
Khi communicate: start with conclusion, then supporting arguments, then details.

**Structure:**
```
ANSWER/RECOMMENDATION (start here)
├── Key Point 1
│   ├── Supporting evidence
│   └── Example
├── Key Point 2
│   └── Supporting evidence
└── Key Point 3
    └── Supporting evidence
```

> **Bài học:** Người trong Western business culture (và ngày càng phổ biến ở VN) want the answer first — tell me what you recommend, then why. Bury answer ở cuối sau nhiều background = lose audience trước khi you get to point. Exception: khi audience là highly skeptical hoặc needs context to evaluate the recommendation — then build case first.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến:** Presentations và memos structuring theo chronological order: "First we researched X, then we found Y, then we analyzed Z, therefore..." Leader muốn: "Here's our recommendation. Here's why. Here's the evidence." Reorder your thinking into top-down structure.
