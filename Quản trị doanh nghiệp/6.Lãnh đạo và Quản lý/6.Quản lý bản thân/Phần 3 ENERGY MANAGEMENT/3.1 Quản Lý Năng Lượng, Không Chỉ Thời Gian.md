### Quản Lý Năng Lượng, Không Chỉ Thời Gian

Jim Loehr & Tony Schwartz ("The Power of Full Engagement"): vấn đề của leaders không phải là không đủ time — đó là không đủ energy. Bạn có thể có 24h nhưng nếu exhausted, output kém.

**4 Dimensions of Energy:**

| Dimension | Depleted khi | Replenished khi |
|---|---|---|
| **Physical** | Sleep deprived, sedentary, poor nutrition | Quality sleep, exercise, nutrition |
| **Emotional** | Constant conflict, lack of connection, suppressed emotions | Positive relationships, authentic expression |
| **Mental** | Constant multitasking, no focused work, information overload | Deep focus blocks, regular breaks, learning |
| **Spiritual** | Work misaligned with values, no meaning | Clear values alignment, meaningful work, contribution |

> **Bài học:** Sustainable high performance không từ working more hours — nó từ managing và recovering energy across 4 dimensions. Top athletes know this: training + recovery = performance. Leader treat themselves như không cần recovery → energy depletion → poor judgment, low empathy, reactive behavior. Recovery is not luxury; it's performance strategy.

> **Phân tích sâu:** **Willpower Depletion** (Roy Baumeister) — self-control và willpower draw từ same finite resource. Khi depleted (by long hours, stress, many decisions), self-regulation fails: người eat worse, react more emotionally, make poorer decisions. This is không weakness of character — nó là physiological reality. Design your day để protect energy for highest-stakes activities.

**Energy management practices:**

1. **Morning routine:** First 60–90 phút không check email/social media. Use for highest-priority, highest-cognitive-demand work.
2. **90-minute work blocks:** Human ultradian rhythm cycles every 90 minutes. Intense focus for 90 min → break → repeat.
3. **Strategic recovery:** 5–10 minute break every 90 minutes, longer lunch, physical movement.
4. **Weekly review:** Sunday evening, 30 minutes — review last week, plan next week, clear mental open loops.
5. **Sleep non-negotiable:** 7–8 hours is not indulgence — it's performance infrastructure. Sleep deprivation impairs judgment similarly to mild intoxication.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến:** Wear exhaustion as badge of honor. "Tôi chỉ ngủ 5 tiếng" framed as dedication. Nhưng sleep-deprived leader make worse decisions, có lower empathy, higher reactivity — harming team và results. Rest is not laziness.
