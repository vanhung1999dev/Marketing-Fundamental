### Emotional Regulation — Kỹ Năng Thực Hành

**Amygdala Hijack** (Goleman): khi threatened, amygdala takes over từ prefrontal cortex → emotional reaction bypasses rational thinking. Trong leadership context: khi criticized, challenged, hoặc stressed, leader có thể react emotionally theo cách damage relationships và credibility.

**Techniques để regulate emotions:**

1. **Name it to tame it** (Dan Siegel) — Label emotion precisely: "Tôi đang feel embarrassed vì my idea was publicly dismissed." Naming activates prefrontal cortex, reduces amygdala activation.

2. **Physiological response:** Deep breathing (box breathing: inhale 4 counts, hold 4, exhale 4, hold 4) shifts autonomic nervous system từ fight-or-flight về calm.

3. **Create space:** "Cho tôi một phút để think về điều này." Trong critical moments, pausing prevents reactive response.

4. **Pre-mortem on your triggers:** Know your triggers in advance — khi nào bạn tend to react poorly? (being publicly criticized? direct challenges to authority? certain personality types?) Pre-knowledge = better preparation.

> **Bài học:** Emotional regulation không phải suppression. "Đừng feel angry" không work — emotion sẽ emerge anyway, often worse. Regulation = feel the emotion + choose your response, rather than be controlled by the emotion. Viktor Frankl: "Between stimulus and response there is a space. In that space lies our freedom and power to choose our response."

> **Sai lầm phổ biến:** Leader believe showing emotion is weakness. Research shows opposite: appropriate emotional expression (genuine excitement, appropriate concern, authentic vulnerability) builds trust và connection. What undermines leadership is uncontrolled emotional reactivity — not emotion itself.
