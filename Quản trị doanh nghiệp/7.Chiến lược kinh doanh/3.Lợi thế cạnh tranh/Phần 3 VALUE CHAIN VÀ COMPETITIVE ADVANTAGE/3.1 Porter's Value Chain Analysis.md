### Porter's Value Chain Analysis

Competitive advantage không phải emerge từ company overall — nó emerge từ specific activities. Value chain phân tích activities trong company để identify where value is created và where advantages lie.

**Primary Activities:**
- Inbound Logistics (receiving, storing inputs)
- Operations (production, transformation)
- Outbound Logistics (delivery to customer)
- Marketing & Sales (customer acquisition)
- Service (after-sale support)

**Support Activities:**
- Firm Infrastructure (management, finance, legal)
- Human Resource Management
- Technology Development
- Procurement

> **Bài học:** Competitive advantage emerge khi company performs certain activities better hoặc cheaper than competitors, OR configures value chain differently than competitors. IKEA's advantage không phải from một activity — nó từ entire value chain configured around self-assembly: flat-pack design (operations) → lower logistics cost (outbound) → large format stores (sales) → customer assembles (eliminating assembly cost). System advantage, không phải single activity advantage.

> **Cạm bẫy:** Outsource activities believed to be "non-core" without examining whether they contribute to competitive differentiation. Amazon did not outsource logistics because logistics is actually their core competitive differentiator — nó không phải "non-core." Before outsourcing, ask: "Is this activity part of how we differentiate?"
