### Chuẩn Bị Cho Nhiều Tương Lai Khác Nhau

Scenario planning không phải predict tương lai — đó là prepare cho range of possible futures.

**Process:**

**Bước 1: Identify Key Uncertainties**

Gì là những uncertainties quan trọng nhất ảnh hưởng đến business trong 3–5 năm? (không phải trends — những gì đã clear direction — mà true uncertainties có thể go either way)

Ví dụ cho startup B2B SaaS:
- Uncertainty A: Regulatory environment cho data privacy (tight vs. loose)
- Uncertainty B: Enterprise adoption of AI tools (fast vs. slow)

**Bước 2: Build Scenarios**

Chọn 2 most critical, most uncertain factors → tạo 2×2 matrix → 4 scenarios.

Mỗi scenario cần: compelling narrative về how world evolved, specific assumptions về key variables, implication cho business.

**Bước 3: Identify Robust Strategies**

*Hedging strategies:* Work well across multiple scenarios (lower risk, potentially lower return)

*Betting strategies:* Excellent trong one scenario, poor trong others (higher risk/reward)

*No-regret moves:* Beneficial regardless of which scenario materializes

> **Bài học:** Scenario planning's biggest value không phải predicting the right scenario — đó là getting leadership team to genuinely grapple with range of futures. Shell Oil (pioneer of scenario planning) không predict oil crisis correctly — nhưng khi crisis happened, their leaders had mentally rehearsed the scenario và responded faster. Mental preparation, not prediction, is the value.

> **Phân tích:** Identify "signposts" — early indicators cho biết which scenario đang materializing. Build monitoring system cho signposts → allows faster strategy pivot khi direction becomes clearer. Waiting until outcome is certain = too late to adapt.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến:** Scenarios được built tại planning retreat rồi forgotten. Scenarios chỉ có value nếu regularly revisited: "Which scenario are we moving toward? What does that mean for our strategy?" Quarterly strategic reviews: check signposts.
