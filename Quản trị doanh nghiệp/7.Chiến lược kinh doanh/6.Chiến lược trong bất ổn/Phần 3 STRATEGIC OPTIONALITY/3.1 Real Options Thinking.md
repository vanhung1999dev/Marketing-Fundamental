### Real Options Thinking

Real options: khả năng make future decisions sau khi có more information — valuable khi uncertainty cao.

**Option to expand:** Invest nhỏ để test, nhưng có khả năng scale quickly nếu results positive
**Option to abandon:** Structured experiments với clear kill criteria — không sunk cost fallacy
**Option to switch:** Maintain flexibility để change approaches if environment shifts

> **Bài học:** Trong high-uncertainty environments, optionality có giá trị riêng. Investment tạo ra option (dù chưa profitable) có thể be wise. Amazon invested in AWS khi đó là not obvious it would be major business — nó tạo ra option. Kindle created option vào digital books. Prime created option vào subscription loyalty. Each small bet preserved future optionality.

> **Phân tích sâu:** **Asymmetric bets** — structure investments để: downside is limited và known (maximum loss = investment), upside is large và uncapped (if it works, returns are multiple of investment). This is how venture capital thinks. Leader should apply same lens to strategic initiatives: "What's maximum we lose if this doesn't work? What's the upside if it does?"
