### Strategy Rhythm — Keeping Strategy Alive

Strategy không phải document — đó là ongoing conversation embedded vào regular operations:

**Annual:** Full strategy review — are we still playing on the right field?

**Quarterly:** OKR review — progress trên key results, adjust priorities nếu cần

**Monthly:** Strategic initiative reviews — are key projects on track?

**Weekly:** Leadership meetings include brief strategic pulse — any signals that require strategy adjustment?

> **Bài học:** Strategy becomes "living" khi nó được referenced regularly in decisions. "Does this hire align với our strategy?" "Does this partnership advance our strategic priority of X?" Leader behavior của referencing strategy in daily decisions = most powerful signal rằng strategy là real, không performative.

> **Cạm bẫy:** Strategic reviews focus entirely trên retrospective (did we hit Q3 numbers?) không phải prospective (what signals are we seeing that should inform strategy adjustment?). Best strategic reviews balance backward accountability và forward intelligence.
