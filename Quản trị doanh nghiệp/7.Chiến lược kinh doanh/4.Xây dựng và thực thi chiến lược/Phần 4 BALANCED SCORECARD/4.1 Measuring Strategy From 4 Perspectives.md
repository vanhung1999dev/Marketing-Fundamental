### Measuring Strategy From 4 Perspectives

Robert Kaplan và David Norton's Balanced Scorecard (BSC) là framework để translate strategy thành measurable objectives across 4 perspectives:

**1. Financial Perspective:** "Làm sao chúng ta trông như thế nào với shareholders?"
- Revenue growth, profitability, cash flow, ROI

**2. Customer Perspective:** "Làm sao customers thấy chúng ta?"
- Customer satisfaction (NPS), retention, market share, customer lifetime value

**3. Internal Process Perspective:** "Chúng ta phải excel ở processes nào?"
- Quality metrics, cycle time, productivity, innovation pipeline

**4. Learning và Growth Perspective:** "Làm sao chúng ta sustain ability to change và improve?"
- Employee engagement, skill development, technology capabilities

> **Bài học:** BSC's insight: doanh nghiệp truyền thống chỉ đo lường financial performance — lagging indicators của past decisions. BSC adds leading indicators: customer satisfaction và internal processes predict future financial performance. Learning & Growth predict future internal process performance. Measurement system phải balance lagging và leading indicators.

> **Phân tích:** Strategy map (visual representation của BSC) shows causal links: "If we improve employee skills (L&G), then internal processes improve, which improves customer satisfaction, which improves financial results." Making these links explicit helps everyone understand how their work connects to business outcomes.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến:** BSC trở thành long list of KPIs across 4 categories mà không clear strategic narrative connecting them. BSC phải tell a story: "This is how our strategy creates value — through these cause-and-effect chains." Metrics phải be selective và connected, không exhaustive.
