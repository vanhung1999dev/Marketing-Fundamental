### S-Curve và Growth Stage Thinking

Mọi growth story follow S-curve:
- **Early stage:** Slow growth (finding product-market fit)
- **Growth stage:** Rapid acceleration (scaling)
- **Maturity:** Growth slows (market saturation, competition)
- **Decline:** Without innovation, starts to shrink

> **Bài học:** Strategy phải differ theo stage. Early stage: experiment để find what works; preserve cash. Growth stage: scale what's working; invest aggressively. Maturity: defend position; efficiency improvements; innovate for next S-curve. Decline: harvest cash; explore new S-curves; sunset gracefully. Applying growth stage strategy to maturity stage (over-invest in declining product) = value destruction.

> **Phân tích:** Leaders phải biết stage của business honestly. Common delusion: business đang trong "temporary slowdown" khi thực ra reaching maturity. Diagnostic: Is growth slowdown due to execution issues (fixable) hoặc market fundamentals (structural)? Honest answer determines strategy.
