### Sales-Led Growth (SLG)

Traditional model: sales team drive growth through direct outreach, relationships, và negotiation.

Works best for: complex enterprise products, high ACV (Annual Contract Value), B2B where relationships critical.

> **Bài học:** SLG và PLG không mutually exclusive — many companies use both. "Product-led sales" = free/self-serve for small customers, direct sales for enterprise. Segment by customer size và complexity: which requires human touch? which can be self-served?

---

**SLG Mechanics — cách hoạt động:**

**Outbound SLG (Proactive):**
```
1. Define ICP (Ideal Customer Profile): industry, company size, pain point
2. Build prospect list: LinkedIn, databases, referrals
3. Outreach: cold email, LinkedIn, cold call
4. Qualify: BANT (Budget/Authority/Need/Timeline) hoặc MEDDIC
5. Discovery: understand problem deeply
6. Demo/Proposal: show solution
7. Negotiate → Close
8. Handoff to Customer Success
```

**Inbound SLG (Reactive):**
```
Marketing generates demand (content, ads, SEO, events)
→ Leads fill form / request demo
→ Sales qualifies (SDR/BDR filter)
→ AE (Account Executive) does discovery và demo
→ Close
→ CS onboards
```

---

**SLG Team Structure:**

| Role | Function | Metric |
|---|---|---|
| **SDR/BDR** (Sales Development Rep) | Outbound prospecting + qualifying inbound | Qualified meetings booked/month |
| **AE** (Account Executive) | Discovery, demo, proposal, negotiation, close | Pipeline, Win rate, ACV |
| **SE** (Sales Engineer) | Technical validation, complex demo | Support AE close rate |
| **CS** (Customer Success) | Onboarding, retention, expansion | NRR (Net Revenue Retention), Churn |
| **RevOps** | Process, tools, data, forecasting | Pipeline accuracy, CRM hygiene |

---

**Key SLG Metrics:**

| Metric | Định nghĩa | Healthy benchmark (B2B SaaS) |
|---|---|---|
| **Pipeline Coverage** | (Total pipeline) / (Quota) | 3–4× quota |
| **Win Rate** | Deals closed won / Total closed deals | 20–30% (higher niche, lower volume) |
| **Average ACV** | Average Annual Contract Value | Depends on segment |
| **Sales Cycle Length** | Days from opp creation to close | SMB: 30–60d; Enterprise: 90–180d |
| **CAC Payback Period** | Months to recover customer acquisition cost | < 18 months ideal |
| **Ramp Time** | Months until new AE at full quota | 3–6 months (SMB), 6–12 (Enterprise) |

---

**SLG vs PLG — Decision Framework:**

| Dimension | SLG | PLG |
|---|---|---|
| **ACV** | High (>$10k/year) | Low-medium (<$10k) |
| **Complexity** | Complex, needs consultation | Can be self-served |
| **Buyer** | Economic buyer (VP, Director, C-suite) | End user drives adoption |
| **Sales cycle** | Long (months) | Short (days-weeks) |
| **Trust required** | High — relationships matter | Low — trial solves trust |
| **Best for** | Enterprise B2B, regulated industries | SaaS, developer tools, productivity |

**Hybrid "Product-Led Sales" (PLS):**
Nhiều modern B2B companies dùng cả hai:
- PLG để acquire individual users (free tier, trial)
- SLG để convert high-usage accounts into enterprise contracts
- CS expansion bán thêm seats/modules into existing accounts

Ví dụ: Slack (free → viral adoption → sales team lands enterprise contracts), Notion (freemium → IT buys enterprise seat), Zoom (free meetings → enterprise security contract).

---

**SLG failure modes phổ biến:**

| Failure | Biểu hiện | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| **No clear ICP** | Sales team pitching mọi người | Define và enforce ICP — say no to out-of-ICP |
| **SDR không qualify** | AE waste time on weak leads | Strict qualification criteria + SDR training |
| **Long sales cycle + no urgency** | Deals sit in pipeline forever | Create urgency (pricing tiers, limited slots, milestone events) |
| **No CS → Churn** | Win customers but can't keep them | Invest in CS before scaling sales |
| **Commission structure misaligned** | Sales close bad-fit customers chasing commission | Include retention metric in comp structure |

> **Phân tích sâu:** David Skok (Matrix Partners) define "SaaS Magic Number" — metric đo lường sales efficiency: (New ARR added this quarter × 4) / (Sales & Marketing spend last quarter). Magic Number > 0.75 = efficient SLG; < 0.5 = broken sales machine. Trước khi scale SLG headcount, đảm bảo Magic Number healthy — adding more reps to a broken process chỉ burns more cash.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến #1:** Hire nhiều AE trước khi có repeatable sales process. "Chúng ta cần 5 AE để hit target" → hire 5 AE → không có clear playbook → 5 AE develop 5 different approaches → learning không compound. Best: founder hoặc first AE documents the process (ICP, discovery questions, demo flow, objection handling) trước khi scale. Hire khi process is proven, not to find the process.

> **Sai lầm phổ biến #2:** Ignore Customer Success khi focused on new sales. Net Revenue Retention (NRR) — retention + expansion — is most important SaaS metric. Company với NRR 120% grows even without new sales. Company với NRR 80% loses 20% revenue/year from churn, tức cần 20% new sales just to maintain flat. CS is not a cost center — it's the engine of efficient growth.

> **Cạm bẫy:** Discount heavily to hit short-term numbers → trains customer to expect discounts → future deals always need discounts → pricing power erosion → margin compression. Discount là addictive và nó creates terrible precedent. Better to lose a deal at full price than win at 50% discount and set that expectation. Track và limit discount frequency và depth.
