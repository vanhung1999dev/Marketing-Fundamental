### Crisis Communication Plan — Prepare Trước Khi Cần

**Crisis Plan Template:**

**1. Crisis team roster**
- Ai trong crisis team?
- Contact details (including off-hours)
- Clear roles: spokesperson, decision-maker, note-taker, social media monitor

**2. Crisis scenarios và playbooks**
- List top 5–10 scenarios most relevant to your business
- Pre-drafted response templates for each
- Decision criteria cho escalation levels

**3. Stakeholder communication map**
- Ai cần được notified? (employees, customers, suppliers, investors, regulators, media)
- In what order?
- Through what channels?

**4. Approved spokespersons**
- Chỉ approved spokespersons speak to media
- Everyone else: "I'm not the right person, let me connect you with [spokesperson]"
- Media training cho spokespersons

> **Sai lầm phổ biến:** Crisis plan exists but không ai biết nó tồn tại, không ai đọc nó, và không ai trained on it. Crisis plan phải be: communicated, drilled (tabletop exercises), và updated annually. Annual "crisis drill" — walk through a scenario — makes the plan real and identifies gaps.
