### Learning From Crisis

Sau khi crisis resolved, formal review là essential:

**Post-crisis review agenda:**
1. **What happened:** Timeline của events
2. **Root cause analysis:** Gì caused the crisis? (5 Whys)
3. **Response assessment:** Gì went well? Gì không?
4. **Gaps identified:** Gì không có và lẽ ra phải có?
5. **Preventive measures:** Gì sẽ prevent similar crisis?
6. **Updated plans:** Crisis plan và risk register cần update gì?

> **Bài học:** Crisis không được wasted nếu được learned from. Companies that do thorough post-crisis reviews emerge more resilient — they understand vulnerabilities và fix them. Companies that treat crisis as aberrations to forget ASAP don't learn and are more vulnerable to next crisis.

> **Phân tích:** Psychological tendency sau crisis: relief khi it's over + desire to move on = skip the review. Leadership phải resist this và insist on structured review. The discomfort of reviewing what went wrong is much smaller than the cost of repeating the crisis.
