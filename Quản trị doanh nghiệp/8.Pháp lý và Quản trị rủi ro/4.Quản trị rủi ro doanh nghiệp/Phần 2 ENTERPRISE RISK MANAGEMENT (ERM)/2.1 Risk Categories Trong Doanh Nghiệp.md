### Risk Categories Trong Doanh Nghiệp

**1. Strategic Risks**
- Rủi ro từ business model, strategy, hay positioning
- Competitors disrupt market, customer needs shift dramatically, business model becomes obsolete
- Management: Strong strategic planning, continuous environmental scanning, business model innovation

**2. Operational Risks**
- Rủi ro từ internal processes, people, systems
- Process failures, human errors, system outages, supply chain disruptions
- Management: SOPs, training, redundant systems, BCP (Business Continuity Plan)

**3. Financial Risks**
- Rủi ro về cash, credit, market, và liquidity
- Customer không trả, cash flow crisis, currency fluctuation, interest rate changes
- Management: Credit policies, cash flow forecasting, hedging, adequate reserves

**4. Compliance và Legal Risks**
- Rủi ro từ regulatory changes, legal disputes, compliance failures
- Tax audits, labor disputes, regulatory violations, contract breaches
- Management: Legal counsel, compliance program, regulatory monitoring

**5. Reputational Risks**
- Rủi ro tổn hại đến brand và trust
- Product failures, ethical violations, social media crisis, employee misconduct
- Management: Strong culture, crisis communication plan, proactive PR

> **Phân tích sâu:** Rủi ro các categories không independent — chúng interconnected. Ví dụ: Operational failure (delivery delay) → Financial risk (contract penalty) → Customer satisfaction risk (churn) → Reputational risk (negative reviews). Risk management phải think về cascade effects, không chỉ isolated risks.
