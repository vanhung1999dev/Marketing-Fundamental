### Tại Sao Culture Là Foundation Của Risk Management

Risk management system tốt nhất thất bại nếu culture suppress bad news:
- Người không report risks vì sợ bị blame
- Managers không escalate problems vì muốn appear "in control"
- "Shoot the messenger" dynamic keeps leadership blind

> **Bài học:** Amy Edmondson's research: Teams với high psychological safety có better safety records (more errors reported → more errors caught and fixed) AND better performance. The same dynamic applies to risk: cultures where bad news travels fast have fewer catastrophic failures because problems are caught early.

**Building Risk-Aware Culture:**

1. **Leader models:** Leader phải be first to raise risks về their own areas — không chỉ ask others to report risks
2. **No blame for honest reporting:** Punish hiding problems, not finding them
3. **Near-miss reporting:** Encourage reporting của close calls và small failures — leading indicators before big failures
4. **Regular risk conversations:** Make risk discussion normal, không special event
5. **Act on reported risks:** Nothing kills risk reporting faster than risks được reported và ignored

> **Cạm bẫy:** Risk management được treated như compliance exercise — fill in the register, present to board, file away. This is worse than no system (false sense of security). Risk management must be embedded in decision-making: "What are the risks?" phải là standard question trong every major decision.
