### Phân Tích Dữ Liệu Và Tối Ưu Delivery Platform

Mỗi delivery platform cung cấp data dashboard cho merchant — nhưng phần lớn quán chỉ nhìn vào total orders và doanh thu. Data sâu hơn có thể reveal những insights hành động ngay: item nào nên remove, giờ nào nên increase capacity, platform nào thực sự profitable sau commission, conversion rate đang fail ở đâu trong funnel.

Delivery platform là marketplace với thuật toán — và thuật toán có thể được hiểu, influenced, và optimized. Merchant có data mindset consistently outperform merchant chỉ "làm và chờ."

---

## Platform Algorithm — Hiểu Để Tối Ưu

Grab, Shopee Food, Baemin đều dùng ranking algorithm để quyết định quán nào được hiển thị cao trong kết quả tìm kiếm của khách. Hiểu algorithm = biết đầu tư effort vào đâu.

**Core ranking factors:**

| Factor | Weight (Ước Tính) | Cách Cải Thiện |
|---|---|---|
| Rating (stars) | Cao | Quality control, respond reviews |
| Order volume | Cao | Promotions, conversion optimization |
| Completion rate | Cao | Không cancel orders sau khi accept |
| On-time rate | Cao | Realistic prep time setting |
| Acceptance rate | Trung bình | Busy mode thay vì reject orders |
| Click-through rate | Trung bình | Ảnh, tên món, description hấp dẫn |
| Promotions running | Trung bình | Tham gia campaigns |

**Compound effect của rating:**
Rating cao → hiển thị cao → nhiều views → nhiều orders → platform reads as "popular" → boost thêm. Virtuous cycle. Ngược lại: rating thấp → visibility giảm → ít orders → harder to recover. Protect rating above all else.

## Performance Metrics — Những Gì Cần Đo

### Delivery Performance

**Completion rate:**
% accepted orders được complete (không cancel). Target: > 95%. Dưới 90% → significant algorithm penalty và customer experience problem.

**Acceptance rate:**
% orders được accept (không decline/reject). Target: > 85%. Nếu bạn đang reject nhiều: busy mode nên được activated sớm hơn thay vì accepting then rejecting.

**On-time rate:**
% orders giao đúng hoặc trước ETA. Target: > 90%. Nếu thấp: review prep time accuracy, kitchen capacity management.

**Average preparation time:**
So sánh với (a) platform-set prep time, (b) competitors tương tự. Nếu actual prep time > set prep time → customers getting later than expected.

### Business Performance

**Conversion rate:**
% người view profile → order. Nếu thấp (< 10%): vấn đề ở menu presentation, pricing, ảnh, hoặc reviews.

**Average Order Value (AOV):**
Giá trị trung bình mỗi order. Thấp so với menu pricing → khách only ordering cheapest items → upsell opportunity.

**Repeat order rate:**
% khách order lần 2+. Key loyalty indicator. Nếu thấp → food/experience không good enough để return.

**Revenue per active hour:**
Revenue ÷ số giờ online. Compare weekday vs. weekend, morning vs. evening. Reveals optimal operating hours.

### Menu Performance

**Best sellers:**
Items contribute most to revenue và order count. Protect these — không change recipe or price without careful analysis.

**Slow movers:**
Items rarely ordered. Questions: wrong price? Bad ảnh? Description unclear? Poor search keywords? Try optimize before remove.

**Add-on attachment rate:**
% orders that include extras/add-ons. Low rate = upsell script opportunity. "Thêm trứng?" hoặc platform-level option prompting.

**Item profitability:**
Revenue from item - ingredient cost - commission - packaging = actual margin per item. Some high-order items may have lower margin than expected.

## Weekly Platform Optimization Routine

Spend 30-45 phút mỗi tuần trên platform data:

**Monday (review previous week):**
- [ ] Performance metrics vs. targets (completion rate, on-time rate, rating)
- [ ] Revenue per platform comparison
- [ ] Top 5 và bottom 5 items by order count
- [ ] Any rating < 4 stars received → respond và add to issue tracker

**Bi-weekly:**
- [ ] Review add-on attachment rate
- [ ] Check if any items need pricing adjustment
- [ ] Review platform promotions available → ROI calculation

**Monthly:**
- [ ] Menu analytics deep-dive
- [ ] Competitive benchmark (look at top 5 competitors trong radius)
- [ ] Photo refresh audit (any photos look outdated or low-quality?)
- [ ] Description và keyword review

## A/B Testing Trên Platform

Không guess về gì works tốt hơn — test.

**What to test:**
- Tên món khác nhau (ảnh hưởng search ranking và conversion)
- Ảnh khác nhau (ảnh product shot vs. lifestyle vs. close-up)
- Description length (short vs. more detailed)
- Pricing (9X,000 vs. 1X0,000 — psychological pricing)

**How to test (nếu platform allows):**
1. Identify one variable to change
2. Set baseline: 2 weeks of current version data
3. Change one variable only
4. Measure 2 more weeks
5. Compare conversion rate, order count, AOV
6. Keep winner, iterate

**If platform doesn't support formal A/B:**
Change và compare before/after with same time period (same day of week, same week type, same weather conditions).

## Platform Profitability Analysis

Revenue không phải profit. Delivery platform economics:

```
Net platform profit = Revenue - Food cost - Packaging - Platform commission - Promo cost
```

**Platform commission rates (approximate):**
- Grab: 25-30%
- Shopee Food: 20-25%
- Baemin: 20-25%

**Full profitability per platform (example):**

| Metric | Grab | Shopee Food |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly revenue | 50 triệu | 30 triệu |
| Commission (27% / 23%) | 13.5 triệu | 6.9 triệu |
| Food cost (30%) | 15 triệu | 9 triệu |
| Packaging | 2 triệu | 1.2 triệu |
| Promo discount | 3 triệu | 1 triệu |
| **Net profit** | **16.5 triệu** | **11.9 triệu** |
| **Net margin** | **33%** | **39.7%** |

In this example: Shopee Food has lower revenue but higher margin percentage. Operational decision may differ from what raw revenue suggests.

## Xương Máu Từ Chiến Trường

> **Bài học #1 — "Grab Mang Về Nhiều Nhất" — Nhưng Profitability Per Order Thấp Nhất**
>
> Một cloud kitchen tập trung 80% effort vào Grab vì "Grab brings the most revenue." Monthly Grab revenue: 60 triệu. Monthly Shopee Food revenue: 25 triệu.
>
> First time doing profitability analysis by platform: Grab commission 28% higher than Shopee. Grab promotions (required to maintain ranking): additional 5% of revenue in discounts. Net Grab margin: 28%. Shopee Food margin (lower volume, fewer promotions needed): 38%.
>
> Grab monthly net: 16.8 triệu. Shopee Food monthly net: 9.5 triệu.
>
> Reallocation: maintain Grab volume but reduce unprofitable promotion frequency. Increase Shopee Food investment (better profile photos, more promotions on Shopee). 3 months later: total net profit up 18% despite Grab revenue down 10%.
>
> **Phân tích sâu:** Revenue metric hides margin reality in delivery. Platforms với higher commission AND requiring more aggressive promotions to maintain ranking erode margins faster. Calculate net margin per platform, not just revenue. This may reveal counterintuitive findings: your "main" platform may not be your most profitable platform. Portfolio allocation should follow profitability, not just volume.

> **Bài học #2 — A/B Testing Tên Món: 35% Conversion Increase Từ Keyword Change**
>
> Một cloud kitchen có item tên "Cơm Rang Đặc Biệt" — low views, low conversion. Nghĩ vấn đề là giá, considered reducing price.
>
> Before reducing price: test tên khác. Research: top search terms in the category. "Cơm rang dương châu", "cơm rang hải sản", "cơm rang tôm" had higher search volume.
>
> Rename to "Cơm Rang Tôm & Mực — Kiểu Dương Châu": same item, clearer description, keyword-rich name.
>
> 3-week test: views up 45%, conversion up 35%, overall orders for item up 60%.
>
> No price change. No recipe change. Just a better name with searchable keywords.
>
> **Phân tích sâu:** Platform is a search engine for food. Menu item names are SEO. Generic names ("Cơm Rang Đặc Biệt") give the algorithm nothing to match against customer searches. Descriptive, keyword-rich names ("Cơm Rang Tôm & Mực — Kiểu Dương Châu") tell the algorithm exactly what the item is and what searches it should appear for. Review your item names: would a customer searching "cơm rang hải sản" find your item? If not, your name needs work. This costs nothing but a name change and delivers significant discovery improvement.

> **Bài học #3 — Peak Hour Data Reveals "Hidden" Revenue: 2 Hours Added Per Day**
>
> A café cloud kitchen analyzed hourly order data for the first time after 6 months of operation. Discovery: significant order spike from 10:30am-12pm (people ordering breakfast at office) that they had been missing because kitchen opened at 11am.
>
> Opening 30 minutes earlier (10:30am): captured early orders. But more significant: data showed another spike at 3-4pm (afternoon snack/coffee run) that they were NOT currently serving — kitchen break during that time.
>
> Adjusted schedule: kitchen break moved from 3-4pm to 2-3pm. Revenue during 3-4pm window: 4-5 triệu/month previously missed.
>
> Total annualized revenue from schedule optimization: 50-60 triệu — without any product, marketing, or pricing change.
>
> **Phân tích sâu:** Operational hours decisions that cost nothing to change can be worth millions. But you only discover this through data. "Bếp break 3-4pm because that's when it's quiet" — maybe it WAS quiet before, but customer behavior may have shifted. Platform hourly order data is the only way to know if you're missing demand you don't even know exists. Pull hourly order data monthly. Look for spikes that don't match your operating hours. Then decide: is the demand worth the operational adjustment?

*Tóm lại từ người trong nghề: Delivery platform data là free intelligence về your business và your customers. Không ai gives you this level of insight in traditional F&B — where you'd have to guess about peak hours, popular items, and conversion rates. Platform merchant dashboard tells you exactly. The discipline is: actually looking at it regularly, knowing what to look for, and being willing to act on what you find. Platform optimization is not a one-time task — it's a weekly practice that compounds over months.*
