### Quản Lý Đơn Hàng Đa Kênh Delivery F&B

Khi bán trên Grab, Shopee Food, Baemin, và nhận order qua Zalo/điện thoại cùng lúc — bếp nhận orders từ 4-5 nguồn khác nhau, mỗi cái với tablet riêng, âm thanh thông báo riêng, và workflow riêng. Nếu không có hệ thống, chaos là không thể tránh: order nhầm, order trễ, items 86 không được sync, và kitchen burn-out.

Paradox của multi-channel: bán nhiều channel hơn không phải lúc nào cũng means more revenue nếu execution quality drops. 5 platform với 60% on-time rate là worse business outcome than 2 platform với 95% on-time rate.

---

## Core Problems Của Multi-Channel Delivery

### Problem 1 — Capacity Không Được Synchronized

Platform A nhận 5 orders. Platform B nhận 4 orders. Platform C nhận 3 orders. Tổng: 12 orders tại cùng thời điểm. Bếp capacity: 8 orders trong 30 phút. Result: 4 orders trễ so với promise time. Platform không biết bếp đã full.

### Problem 2 — Menu Không Sync Khi 86 Item

Item hết giữa service. Ai đó phải manually pause item trên từng platform riêng lẻ: Platform A → Platform B → Platform C. Giữa bước 1 và bước 3: 2-3 orders đã được nhận từ platform B và C. Những orders này phải bị cancel — customer experience tệ, platform penalty potential.

### Problem 3 — Priority Không Rõ Ràng

Khi có 12 orders cùng lúc: order nào làm trước? Theo platform? Theo order time? Theo promised delivery time? Không có system → mỗi cook quyết định independently → inconsistent prioritization → some orders late, some early.

### Problem 4 — Error Tracking Phân Tán

Complaint từ platform nào? Về order nào? Ai đã làm? Không có central record → impossible to identify patterns, improve systematically.

## Solution Options Theo Volume

### Option 1 — Manual Multi-Tablet (Volume Thấp: < 20 Orders/Giờ)

**Setup:**
- 1 dedicated tablet/monitor per platform
- 1 designated "order receiver" person (separate from cooking)
- Physical whiteboard hoặc order ticket system để queue và prioritize
- Verbal confirmation protocol với bếp

**Priority rule:**
Sort by promised delivery time, không phải order time. Order với earliest promised delivery time → done first.

**Capacity management:**
Order receiver tracks open orders count. Khi approaching capacity: manually set platforms to "Busy" mode.

**Cost:** Almost zero additional cost beyond existing tablets.

**Limitation:** Error-prone, human-dependent, fails during extreme busy periods.

### Option 2 — Aggregator System (Volume Trung Bình-Cao: > 20 Orders/Giờ)

**What it does:**
- Pull orders từ tất cả platforms vào 1 unified dashboard
- Single screen cho order receiver
- 86 một item → auto-pause trên tất cả platforms simultaneously
- Order priority queue managed in single interface
- Route orders đến kitchen display hoặc printer

**Vietnam options:**
- BEE System, iPos, Lobibox, iPOS.vn với delivery integration
- International: Otter, Deliverect (more expensive but more features)

**Investment:** Monthly subscription fee, typically 500K-2 triệu/tháng depending on features.

**ROI:** Eliminates 86-sync errors, reduces training needed, enables higher volume operation.

### Option 3 — Full POS + KDS Integration

**For high-volume operations:**
POS system với built-in delivery aggregation. Orders flow directly to Kitchen Display System (KDS) — no printed tickets, no verbal communication required.

**Benefits:**
- Real-time order status visible to all stations
- Automatic priority queue
- Timer alerts for orders approaching promised ETA
- Complete order history and performance data

**Cost:** Higher upfront investment but lowest error rate.

## Capacity Management — Không Ôm Nhiều Hơn Xử Lý Được

### Calculate Actual Kitchen Capacity

Kitchen capacity test: during a normal busy period, what's the maximum number of orders per hour you can handle while maintaining quality AND on-time rate > 90%?

This is your true capacity ceiling. Set maximum accepted orders/hour accordingly.

**How to implement capacity limits:**

**"Busy mode" on platforms:**
All delivery platforms have a "pause" or "busy" mode — no new orders accepted when activated. Assign responsibility: order receiver activates busy mode when queue reaches 80% of capacity.

**Rule:** Better to serve 30 orders perfectly than 50 orders mediocre. Review of 1 star for late delivery is more damaging than not accepting the order.

**Auto-busy schedule:**
Platforms allow setting estimated prep times. Set prep time slightly longer during peak hours → platform shows customers longer ETA → filters some demand naturally.

## Kitchen Display System (KDS) Cho Delivery

KDS là màn hình (thường tablet hoặc monitor) tại mỗi cooking station hiển thị orders real-time.

**Key features for delivery:**
- Priority sorted by promised delivery time (earliest ETA first)
- Delay alert: visual/audio warning khi order sắp trễ so với ETA
- Station-specific view: hot station thấy hot items, cold station thấy cold
- Order status: new → in progress → ready → picked up

**Benefits vs. paper ticket:**
- No lost tickets
- Real-time priority update khi new orders arrive
- No verbal communication required (fewer errors)
- Performance data automatically captured

## 86 Protocol — Critical Moment

Khi item hết trong service:

**Step 1 (Ngay lập tức):** Notify order receiver — "86 [item]"

**Step 2 (Trong 30 giây):** Order receiver pauses item on ALL platforms simultaneously

**Step 3 (Kiểm tra):** Any orders already received for this item? Process as cancellation with explanation or substitute with customer consent.

**Step 4 (Sau service):** Investigate why ran out → update par level → prevent recurrence.

**Critical: sequence matters.** Step 2 phải trước step 3. Orders still incoming while you're handling existing ones is avoidable chaos.

## Xương Máu Từ Chiến Trường

> **Bài học #1 — 5 Platform, 3-Star Average: More Channels = More Problems**
>
> Một cloud kitchen launch với all 5 available platforms simultaneously — "more channels = more revenue." Revenue trong tháng 1: 60 triệu. Rating: 3.2 stars average.
>
> Investigation: on-time rate hanya 55%. Error rate (wrong items, missing items): 8%. Complaint rate: 12% of orders.
>
> Decision: cut xuống 2 platforms (Grab và Shopee Food, highest volume), focus kitchen capacity on serving those well.
>
> Month 2 (2 platforms): revenue 45 triệu (lower). Rating: 4.4 stars. On-time: 89%. Error rate: 1.5%.
>
> Month 3 (2 platforms, now with improved operation): revenue recovered to 58 triệu. Month 4: 72 triệu. Rating maintained at 4.3-4.5.
>
> **Phân tích sâu:** Platform ratings compound — high ratings attract more orders via algorithm. Low ratings suppress visibility. Starting on 5 platforms simultaneously với underdeveloped operations gets you bad ratings on all 5 — very hard to recover. Better strategy: start with 1-2 platforms, build ratings to 4.5+, then expand to additional platforms. Reputation on established platforms then spills over to new ones. Thứ tự matter: competence first, expansion second.

> **Bài học #2 — Không Có 86 Sync: 23 Orders Cùng 1 Item Bị Cancel Trong 1 Buổi**
>
> Cloud kitchen, Saturday peak. Chicken thighs ran out at 12:30pm. Staff member manually paused on Grab (platform 1). Forgot about Shopee Food and Baemin.
>
> Next 45 minutes: 14 orders received on Shopee Food, 9 orders on Baemin — all with chicken thigh. Kitchen can't fulfill. Mass cancel with "item unavailable" explanation.
>
> Result: 23 cancellations, 18 of which resulted in immediate 1-star reviews. Platform cancellation ratio spiked → algorithm suppressed listing → weekend traffic dropped 40% the following week.
>
> Fix: aggregator system installed. 86 on aggregator = automatic pause across all platforms simultaneously. Zero recurrence in 6 months.
>
> **Phân tích sâu:** The cost of aggregator system (1-2 triệu/month) paid for itself in preventing 1 incident of this scale. One 23-cancellation incident cost more in reviews and algorithm suppression than 6 months of subscription fees. This is a system problem with a system solution. Manual process of "remember to update all platforms" fails under peak pressure — exactly when you need it most. If you're on multiple platforms, aggregation is not optional; it's risk management.

> **Bài học #3 — Capacity Management: "Chúng Mình Nhận Hết, Làm Trễ Thêm Một Chút"**
>
> Kitchen policy: never reject orders. "Làm trễ một chút nhưng làm hết." During Friday peak: 45 orders nhận trong 1 tiếng, kitchen capacity realistically 25-30.
>
> Result: average delay 22 phút so với promised ETA. 60% of orders arrived late. That Friday: 12 negative reviews về late delivery. Platform warning issued for on-time rate below 80%.
>
> Following Friday: implement capacity management. Set max 28 orders/giờ. At 22 orders in queue: activate "busy" mode across all platforms.
>
> Result: 28 orders completed, 96% on-time, 2 negative reviews (down from 12). Revenue slightly lower (28 vs. 45 orders) but net customer satisfaction significantly higher.
>
> Month-over-month: higher rating means higher algorithm ranking means more organic traffic. Revenue recovered and exceeded "accept everything" level by month 3.
>
> **Phân tích sâu:** "Nhận hết" is counterproductive math. In delivery, 45 orders delivered late = 45 customers who potentially give 1-star reviews. 28 orders delivered on-time = 28 customers who potentially return and leave 4-5 star reviews. The revenue difference is 17 orders. The experience difference is massive. Delivery platforms are rating-driven marketplaces: your visibility and attractiveness to new customers is directly correlated to your rating. Every late delivery is a rating risk. Capacity management protects the rating that drives all future revenue.

---

*Tóm lại từ người trong nghề: Multi-channel delivery không phải là về being everywhere — đó là về executing well wherever you are. Start with 1-2 platforms, build your systems (86 sync, capacity management, quality control), achieve 4.5+ rating, then expand. Aggregator system is required investment for anyone running 3+ platforms or 30+ orders/hour. Capacity management is required discipline for everyone — knowing your limit and staying within it is what separates operations that compound quality reputation from those that drown in mediocre execution.*
